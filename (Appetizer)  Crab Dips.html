Appetizer: Crab Dips

This is from Eleanor Haley, St. Petersburg, FL, around 1980.

 

Hot Crab Dip

 

8 oz. cream cheese

small onion, grated

1 can crab meat, drained

1/2 t. worchestershire

1 t. lemon juice

dash tabasco

salt and pepper to taster

slivered almonds

 

Combine all ingredients.  Sprinkle top with almonds.  Bake 25 - 30 minutes at 350.  Serve with crackers or Melba toast.
