Appetizer: Cheese Ball

This is from Helen Shoemaker, St. Petersburg, FL in the late 1970's.

 

Cheese Ball

 

12 oz. cream cheese

4 oz. bleu cheese

8 oz. sharp Cheddar cheese 

8 oz. Swiss cheese

1 tsp. Worcestershirse sauce

1/2 tsp. salt

1 tsp. garlic salt

1 cup pecans; 3/4 cup chopped fine, 1/4 cup a bit larger for outside of ball


Grate cheese and let it come to room temperature.  Mix all ingredients above except 1/4 cup pecans.  Add 3/4 cup chopped pecans.  Spread 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley and 1/4 cup chopped pecans on large sheet of wax paper.  Shape cheese into ball and roll in parsley and pecans until covered.


Let stand in refrigerator overnight.  Can be frozen indefinitely.  


Serve with assorted crackers; Triscuits are the best.
