Appetizer: Deviled Eggs

Mom left us a cutting from the AJC that told us how she made her deviled eggs.  I made them Easter, 2008 and they were eaten!  The egg cooker is a wonderful tool for hard boiling!

 

Mom's Deviled Eggs 

 

6 or 7 hard boiled eggs

3+ Tbs. mayonnaise (for 6 eggs)

1 tsp. mustard

pinch of salt

pinch of sugar

paprika for garnish

 

Cut the eggs in half and mash with a fork.  Combine with other ingredients except paprika.  Add more mayo and spices as needed.

 

Finish with paprika.
