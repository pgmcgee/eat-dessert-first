Appetizer: Dried Beef Dip

We don't use much dried beef anymore. This recipe is from Gail in St. Petersburg, FL, about 1980. It must have been good because it was written down several times!!  That's why it's included here.   See what you think.  


Dried Beef Dip

 

1 8 oz. pkg. sour cream

1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese

4 scallions, sliced

1 small jar dried beef

Chopped nuts as a garnish


Soften cream cheese.  Mix all ingredients.  Add garnish.  Heat in microwave or oven.
