Appetizer: Gouda Cup

Gouda Cup

 

1 - 12 oz. Gouda cheese

3 tbsp. beer

1 tsp. steak sauce

dash liquid red pepper seasoning

 

Cut a circle in red wax top of Gouda cheese and peel off cut section.

 

Scoop out cheese, leaving red covering and 1/4" cheese shell.  Refrigerate shell.

 

Let scooped out cheese soften at room temperature.  

 

Mash cheese with fork or blender.  Blend with remaining ingredients.  Let stand an hour to blend flavors.

 

Pile cheese mix into chilled Gouda cup.  Shape top to look like a cone.  

 

Cover with Saran or foil and refrigerate.  

 

Flavor is improved if mi is allowed to age a few days.  

 

To serve:  Let cheese cup come to room temperature.  Surround with pumpernickel triangles or crackers.
