Appetizer: Meatballs

This is Eleanor Haley's recipe enjoyed by all in St. Pete; circa 1980.

 

Miniature Meatballs

 

1.5 lbs. ground beef

3/4 c. Quaker or Mother's oats (quick or old fashioned, uncooked)

1/2 t. salt

1/2 t. garlic salt

1/4 t. pepper

1/4 c. finely chopped onion

2 T milk


Combine all ingredients  Shape to form 1" meatballs.  Pan fry in small amount of shortening.  Dip in sauce made by combining the following ingredients:


8 oz. can tomato sauce

1 T vinegar

2 T brown sugar

1 T worcestershire sauce

 

 

The next recipe is from the same era.  It's from Gail from Euclid, around 1980.

 

Cocktail Meatballs

 

2 large cans tomato sauce

2 large cans tomato paste

4 cans water

Cook above 3 - 4 hours.  Add some salt and pepper to taste.

 

Meatballs:

4 lbs. ground beef

1/2 lb. bulk pork sausage

6 eggs

2 c. bread crumbs

1 1/3 c. Parmesan cheese

Salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano, and onion salt to taste.

 

Make small balls and drop in sauce.  Do not brown balls first.
