Appetizer: Mexican Dip

There are lots of Mexican dip recipes now.  This one is from the late 1970's and is our family favorite.

 

 

Mexican Dip

 

Brown ground beef with Taco Seasoning Mix.  Follow package directions for quantity of meat.

Layer:

 

1 can refried beans

seasoned beef

salsa

carton of sour cream

lettuce 

grated cheddar cheese

scallions

chopped tomatoes

 

Make several hours ahead of serving time.


Serve with tortilla chips.
