Appetizer: Sombrero Dip

This one is from Marie Zachary,  It's very good!  Circa late 1970's

 

Sombrero Dip

 

2 lb. hamburger

2 c. onion

2 c. hot catsup

4 T. chili powder

salt to taste

 

Brown hamburger and onions.  Drain.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer about 15 minutes.

 

Garnish for above:

 

sharp cheddar cheese, grated

chopped onions

chopped ripe olives

 

Keep warm and serve with corn chips.
