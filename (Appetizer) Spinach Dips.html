Appetizer: Spinach Dips

This is a classic from Ann Armstrong, June 23, 1980, St. Petersburg, FL.  That date is Mom and Dad's anniversary.  I wonder if they were with the Armstrong's that day.

 

Spinach Dip

 

1 10 oz. frozen, chopped spinach

   Thaw and drain well.

3 T. mayonnaise

1 T. minced onion

1 c. sour cream

1 .04 pkg. Ranch House Original Dressing

 

Mix all ingredients and let stand at least 3 - 4 hours.

 

 

Here's another one from Mary Lou Kimmel, about the same time frame.  The seasoning packet is different.

 

Spinach Dip

 

1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach

1 c. mayonnaise

1 medium onion, chopped fine

1 c. sour cream

1 pkg. Knorr dried vegetable soup mix

1 can water chestnuts, chopped fine

 

Thaw spinach; pat dry with paper towels.  Mix all ingredients.  Chill for at least 3 hours.  Serve with crackers.
