Mom wrote that this was given to her by Peg Shoemaker.

 

Ann's Artichoke Dip

 

1 - 14 oz. artichoke hearts

1 c. light mayonnaise

1 c. freshly grated Parmesan cheese

1 c. mozzarella cheese, grated

2 T. parsley

1 dash garlic (jar fresh garlic)

 

Pre-heat oven to 350.  Use a 9" pie plate.

 

Chop artichoke hearts, place in bowl and mix in all the other "stuff".  Put it all in the baking dish and bake 25 to 30 minutes.
