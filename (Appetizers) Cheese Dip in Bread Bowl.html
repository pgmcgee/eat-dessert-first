Appetizers: Cheese Dip in Bread Bowl

This one is from Ann Batteiger when her last name was Spies!!

 

Ann's Cheese Dip in Bread Bowl

 

1 extra large round bread

2 - 3 oz. blue cheese

3 Kraft nippy cheese tubes

1 can beer

 

Slice top of bread and scoop out inside to make bowl for cheese dip.   Tear scooped out bread into chunks for dipping.


Bring beer to boil to eliminate foam; cool it.  Combine softened cheeses, add beer gradually.  Beat until smooth, light and runny.  (You will use approximately half of the beer.)


Mixture thickens after it sits in the bread bowl.  Do not refrigerate.  Let set at room temperature until ready to use.
