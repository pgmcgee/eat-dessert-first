Beef: Crock Pot Italian Beef

Mom copied this recipe in July, 2005 while at the Batteiger's, Jacksonville

 

Crock Pot Italian Beef

 

1-3lb. chuck roast

1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning

2 Tbsp. garlic powder

1 1/2c. frozen diced onions

1 - 12 oz. jar roasted red peppers (drained)

2 c. roasted pepper and onion pasta sauce

seasoned salt to taste

 

Combine all ingredients in crock pot.  Serve over split Chicago hard rolls with French fries.
