Bread: Banana Muffins

This recipe is from Rosemarie Gore, widow of Mom's brother, Jack Gore.

 

Banana Muffins

 

2 c. flour                                                             1/2 tsp. cinnamon

1 tsp. baking powder                                            1/4 tsp. vanilla

1 tsp. baking soda                                               1/2 c. butter

1/4 tsp. salt                                                         1 c. brown sugar

1/2 tsp. nutmeg                                                   2 eggs

1/2 tsp. ginger                                                     2 very ripe bananas


Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and spices.  Set aside.


Cream butter with sugar; beat in eggs and mashed bananas.  Gradually blend in flour mixture.  


When mixed well, fold in raisins (1/2 cup and/or nuts).


Fill greased muffin tins 1/2 full.  Bake at 350 for 35 - 40 minutes.
