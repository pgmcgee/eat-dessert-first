Bread: Monkey Bread

This is from Ann Spies Batteiger, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Monkey Bread

 

3 cans biscuits

1/2 c. sugar

2 T. cinnamon

 

Grease bundt pan.  Mix sugar and cinnamon.  Cut biscuits in quarters and coat with sugar and cinnamon mixture.  Place in bundt pan.

 

Next:  1/2 cup butter, 3/4 cup sugar*, 1 tsp. vanilla.

Mix all ingredients and boil until sugar dissolves.  Pour over biscuits and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

 

Turn onto plate immediately.

 

*Use left over sugar and cinnamon mixture from step 1 and add sugar to make 3/4 cup.  This gives it a more "cinnamony" taste!
