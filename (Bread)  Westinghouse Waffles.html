Bread: Westinghouse Waffles

This is a recipe from the University of Miami era before Mom married.

 

Betsy Gore's Old Westinghouse Waffles

 

2 cups flour

4 t. baking powder

1/4 t. salt

2 T. sugar

2 eggs

1 1/4 cups sweet milk

6 T. melted fat (Wesson Oil)

 

Mix and sift dry ingredients.  Beat egg yolks.  Add milk and oil to egg. Fold into dry ingredients.  Do not beat.
