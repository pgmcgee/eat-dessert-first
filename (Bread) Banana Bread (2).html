Bread: Banana Bread (2)

This banana bread is named for Dad.  It's dated Fall, 2001 from the Bisquick Box!


Skip's Banana Bread


1 1/3 bananas

2/3 c. sugar (1/3 sugar, 1/3 Splenda)

1/4 c. milk

3 T. oil

1/2 tsp. vanilla

3 eggs

2 2/3 c. Bisquick


Oven at 350 degrees

Grease pan

Mash bananas

Stir bananas, sugar, milk, oil, and eggs in bowl.  Stir in Bisquick.  Pour in pan.  Bake 50 - 60 minutes.
