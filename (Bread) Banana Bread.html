Bread: Banana Bread

This is Paul Deen's recipe.  

 

Banana Bread

 

Mix in bowl by hand:

 

1 stick butter at room temperature

1 c. sugar

1/2 tsp. salt

2 eggs 

1 tsp. vanilla 

1 1/2 c. flour, sifted

1 tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. baking powder

3 ripe bananas, mashed

 

Preheat oven to 350.  Grease 9 x 5" loaf pan.  Combine butter and sugar, mix well.  Add salt, eggs, vanilla, flour, soda, baking powder and bananas.  Mix well.  Pour batter in pan.  Bake for 50 minutes.
