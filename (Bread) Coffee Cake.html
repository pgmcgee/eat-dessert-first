Bread: Coffee Cake

This is a Marie Zachary recipe, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Coffee Cake

 

2 c. sifted flour

2 sticks butter

1 c. sugar

1 c. brown sugar, firmly packed

1 tsp. cinnamon

dash salt

 

Cut butter in above ingredients to a coarse meal texture.  Reserve 1 c. for topping.

 

Add 2 well beaten eggs, 1/2 tsp. baking soda and 1 small carton sour crean.  

 

Butter a 9x13" or two 8x8" pans.

 

Spread 1 c. pecans, add mixture and top with reserved mixture.

 

Bake for 35 minutes at 350.
