Bread: Corn Bread Sticks

This is from the University of Miami Collection; pre-1950.

 

Corn Bread Sticks

 

1 egg

1 c. milk

1 tbsp. melted shortening

2 c. meal

2 tsp. baking powder

3/4 tsp. salt

 

Beat egg, add milk and melted shortening.  Add to this sifted dry ingredients.  Beat well.  Bake in hot, greased tins - 450 and bake for 15 - 20 minutes.  

 

Makes about 12 sticks.
