Bread: Cornbread Dodgers

This is in Cathy Batteiger Moss' handwriting.  These are the dodgers for turnip greens.

 

Dodgers

 

1 cup cornmeal (self-rising)

2 T. flour

chopped onion

 

Use liquid from turn greens to mix.  (Add boiled water to greens if more liquid is needed.)  Sprinkle each dodger with pepper.
