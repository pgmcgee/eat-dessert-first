Bread: Cornbread

Here's a couple more cornbread recipes.  The first one is from University of Miami Home Ec. days.  The second is from Southern Living, 1999.

 

Cornbread I

 

1 cup meal

1/2 t. salt

1/4 t. baking powder

1/2 cup buttermilk

1/2 t. soda

1 T. bacon drippings

 

Mix and bake at 350.

 

 

Cornbread II

 

2 c. self-rising Buttermilk white cornmeal mix

1/2 c. flour (all purpose)

1/4 c. butter, melted

1 large egg, lightly beaten

2 c. buttermilk


Heat 9" skillet (well greased) at 450 degrees for 5 minutes.  


Stir all ingredients.  Pour batter into hot skillet.  


Bake at 450 for 20 minutes or until golden.  


Makes about 5 c. crumbled
