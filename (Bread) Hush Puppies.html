Bread: Hush Puppies

This is circa 1955.  Mom wrote "good" on the card.  There is a second, similar recipe below that was copied at a later date.

 

Hush Puppies

 

2 c. meal

1 tbsp. flour

1 tsp. baking powder

1 1/2 tsp. salt

1 whole egg

6 tbsp. chopped onion

1 1/2 c. (scant) milk

 

Mix all dry ingredients, add chopped onion, then milk and egg.

 

Drop into deep fish grease.   

 

 

Hush Puppies II

 

 1 1/2 c. yellow meal

1 c. self-rising flour

2 T. cooking oil (Wesson)

1 T. baking powder

1 tsp. salt or to taste

1 tsp. sweet basil

1/4 c. chopped onions

Milk to the consistency of pancake batter


Use a tablespoon to dip into hot fat.
