Bread: Marie's Cornbread

This is Marie Zachary's recipe, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Marie's Cornbread

 

2 eggs, well beaten

1/2 c. Wesson oil

1 small carton sour cream

1 small can cream style corn

1 c. meal (fine ground)

1 T. baking powder

1 1/2 tsp. salt

1 tsp. sugar

 

Mix and bake at 375 for 30 - 40 minutes in a well greased 8" pan.
