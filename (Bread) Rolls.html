Bread: Rolls

This is a recipe written by Nana.  There are no directions as to how to form the rolls!  Mom notes that she had to adjust the oven temperature.  The recipe calls for 425 degrees.  Mom wrote, "400 degrees too hot for my oven - Bake at 375" and dated the comment March 22, 1973.

 

Becky's Rolls

 

Cream 1/3 cup sugar and 1/3 cup Crisco.  Add 1 cup boiling water and let stand until cool.  

 

Dissolve 1 yeast packet in 1/4 cup warm water.  Add 1 well beaten egg and yeast to above.

  

Mix together 3 1/2 cups plain flour, 1 tsp. salt, 1/4 tsp. baking powder, and 1/4 tsp. baking soda.  Add to liquid mixture.  Let rise 20 minutes.  

 

Put in refrigerator and let stand 2 hours.  


Let the rolls rise 2 hours.  

 

Butter tops of rolls before baking. 


Bake in oven at 425 for 20 minutes or until light brown. .


Makes 30 rolls.
