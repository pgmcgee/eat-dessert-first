Bread: Sourdough Biscuits

This is from Jo Krauel, Sue's friend in the 70's. She was a great friend to Mom and Dad also.  We had some wonderful times.  We lost touch and I tried to find her for the 50th Anniversary party but was unsuccessful.  The date of her writing is 5/19/76.   I made these for a long time and they are good!!  Jo notes that she "does not feed the starter this often.  It really depends on how you use it."


Sourdough Biscuits


Starter: 
 

Feed the starter every 5 to 7 days with:


1 cup flour

1 cup milk

1/4 cup sugar


Keep refrigerated; tightly covered.



Biscuits:


1 cup starter dough

1 cup flour

1/4 cup cooking oil

2 t. baking powder

1/2 t. salt

1/4 t. soda


Mix well.  Turn out on floured surface and knead.  Cut biscuits out with desired cutter.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 425 to 450.
