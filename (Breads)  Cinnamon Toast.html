Breads: Cinnamon Toast

From the University of Miami collection.  I believe the "flame" would be the broiler today. 

 

Cinnamon Toast

 

Slice bread 1 inch thick.  Cut in triangles.  Toast on both sides in preheated oven.

 

Spread with butter, then sprinkle on a mixture made of:  2 tbsp. cinnamon to 1 cup sugar.  

 

Place under flame until sugar melts. 

 

Serve hot.
