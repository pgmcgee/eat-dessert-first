Breads: Cheese Bread

This is from Roxanne, Mom and Dad's next door neighbor at the lake.

 

Cheese Bread

 

2 French bread loaves cut into 1 1/2" slices

1 large hunk New York sharp cheddar cheese, grated

1 large hunk blue cheese.broken up

1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

2 sticks butter, softened


Mix Parmesan cheese with softened butter.  Add cheddar and blue cheeses.  Mix all well and store overnight in refrigerator.


When ready to prepare the bread, remove cheese mixture and let it stand until it returns to room temperature.  


Apply mixture thickly to bread top.  Put on cookie sheet and broil on second level of oven with door ajar.  Watch bread so it does not burn.


Cool 1 minute before serving.
