Breads: Corn Bread

This is a recipe Cathy shared with Mom on August 31, 1989.  Mom writes, "This is Ruth Moss' way of making corn bread - delicious!".

 

Cathy's Corn Bread  


Approx. 1 1/4 c. self-rising corn meal

About 1 c. milk with a dash of cider vinegar 

Nearly 1 T. mayonnaise

1 large egg

 

Mix all and pour into greased (cooking spray okay) iron skillet.
