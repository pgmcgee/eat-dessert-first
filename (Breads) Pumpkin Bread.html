Breads: Pumpkin Bread

This is from Kay Palmeri, Wekiva during Mom and Dad's years in Orlando, FL


Pumpkin Bread

 

2 teaspoons nutmeg

1 teaspoon cinnamon

pinch cloves

3 cups sugar

1 cup oil

4 eggs, lightly beaten

1 1/2 teaspoons salt

1 cup pumpkin

2/3 cup water

2 teaspoons baking soda

3 cups sifted flour

 

Combine and blend nutmeg, cinnamon, sugar, oil, eggs, and salt.  Stir in pumpkin and water.  Add soda and sifted flour and mix until well blended.

 

Spread into 2 well buttered and floured 9x5x3" loaf pans.  

 

Bake in preheated 350 oven for 1 hour.  

 

Can add nuts and raisins.

Pumpkin Bread

 

Oven Temp:  350

Time:  1 hour


Grease 2 bread pans.  Cream together 2/3 cup shortening and 2 2/3 cups sugar.  Mix in 4 eggs.  Blend in 2 cups pumpkin puree and 2/3 cup water.


In separate bowl, mix together:


3 1/3 cup flour

1/2 tsp. baking powder

2 tsp. baking soda

1 1/2 tsp. salt

1 tsp. cinnamon 

1 tsp. nutmeg

3/4 tsp. ground cloves

 

Mix dry ingredients into pumpkin mixture well.  Fold in 2/3 cup chopped nuts and 2/3 cup chopped dates.

 

Bake until lightly browned and toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.

 

Enjoy with butter or cream cheese.
