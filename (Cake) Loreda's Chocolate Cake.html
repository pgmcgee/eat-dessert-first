Cake: Loreda's Chocolate Cake

Mom wrote that this one is "Good!!".  It is dated 9/15/63 and is written on the back of a drink ticket from a convention!!


Chocolate Cake


1 box Milk Chocolate Cake Mix (Betty Crocker)


No further instructions for the cake!!  This one is all about the icing!


Icing


1 box 10X sugar

4 or 5 tbsp. cocoa (+ or -)

1/2 stick butter

1 tbsp. Wesson Oil                   Vanilla

1 tbsp. milk (+ or -)


Melt butter in large pan then add others and stir (over low heat) until sugar dissolves.  Add vanilla.
