Dessert: Banana Bread

This recipe is from Elsie Caldwell, February 7, 1984.  Mom notes:  "This is delicious - Elsie makes it and slices it very thin and puts cream cheese between two slices - great!".

 

Note the way the recipe is written; contents and instructions are lumped together.  

 

It reminds me of the nut bread with cream cheese we all liked at Mom's Memorial Celebration.  Enjoy!

 

Banana Bread

 

Cream together:  1 1/2 c. sugar and 1/2 c. oleo until sugar dissolves.

 

Add:  2 eggs and mix

 

Add:  4 ripe bananas and mix

 

Add:  1 c. chopped nuts (pecans or walnuts)

         2 c. flour

         pinch of salt

         1/2 tsp. baking soda

 

Mix well. Grease pan.  Bake in 350 oven for about an hour.
