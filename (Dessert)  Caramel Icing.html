Dessert: Caramel Icing

This is from Mom's University of Miami days.  

 

2 2/3 c. brown sugar

1/2 c. butter

1 c. cream or rich milk

 

Cream butter, sugar and stir while cooking over medium low heat until smooth and creamy.  Add 1 cup cream or milk and cook until it will form a soft ball when dropped into cold water.  


Remove from heat and cool for about 10 minutes.  Beat until thick enough to spread.
