Dessert: Impossible Pumpkin Pie

Impossible Pumpkin Pie

 

3/4 c. sugar

1/2 c. Bisquick

2 tbsp. margarine

1 cn (13 oz) evaporated milk

2 eggs

1 can (16 oz) pumpkin

2 1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice

2 tsp. vanilla

 

Heat oven to 350.  Grease 9 - 10" pie plate.  

 

Beat all ingredients until smooth.  Pour into pie plate.

 

Bake until golden brown and knife inserted in centers comes out clean; about 50 - 55 minutes.  

 

Garnish with whipped cream if desired.
