Dessert: Molasses Cookies

This recipe is from Aunt Lula, Nana's sister.  Harry and Billy Jeter are Lula's sons.  They attended Mom's memorial service.  Billy lives in the Raleigh, NC area and Harry lives in the Columbia, SC region.

 

Lula's Molasses Cookies

 

1 cup melted shortening

1 cup dark molasses

1 egg, well beaten

4 1/2 cups sifted plain flour

2 tsp. ginger

2 tsp. cinamon

1/4 tsp. cloves

1 tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. salt

1/2 cup sugar

 

Combine shortening and molasses; add egg and stir well.  

 

Mix and sift dry ingredients; stir in gradually.

 

Chill dough until firm enough to roll.  Roll 1/8" thick and cut into 2" rounds.

 

Bake at 375.
