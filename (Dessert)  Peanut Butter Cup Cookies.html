Dessert: Peanut Butter Cup Cookies

This is sure to be a favorite of Aunt Sue!!

 

Peanut Butter Cup Cookies

 

1 pkg. Betty Crocker Peanut Butter Cookies  

1 pkg. Reese Peanut Butter candies


Roll dough into 1" balls.  Put into miniature muffin pans lined with paper muffin cups.  


Bake at 350 for 12 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Press a peanut butter cup into the center of each cookie.  Bake for 3 more minutes.  Remove from oven and cool completely in pans on wire racks.
