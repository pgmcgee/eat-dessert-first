Dessert: "Dump" Pound Cake

This is Mary Dickerson's "dump" cake.  Nana never really approved of it because it wasn't made like a "true" pound cake.  I'm liking how easy it sounds!  Think I'll make one to have with ambrosia!!

 

Pound Cake

 

1 stick margarine at room temperature

5 large eggs

1 c. Crisco

1 c. whole milk

2 1/2 c. sugar

1 c. self-rising flour

2 c. plain flour

1 tsp. vanilla

2 or more tsp. lemon flavoring

 

Place all ingredients in mixer and beat on cream setting for 10 minutes.  

 

Pour into greased and floured tube pan.

 

Bake for one hour and fifteen minutes at 350.
