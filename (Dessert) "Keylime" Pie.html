Dessert: "Keylime" Pie

This is Cathy Batteiger Moss' recipe.

 

 Cathy's "Keylime" Pie


1 small can limeade or lemonade

1 can Eagle sweetened condensed milk

1 container Cool Whip

1 large or 2 small graham cracker crust


Let limeade or lemonade thaw and mix well with condensed milk.  Fold in Cool Whip and pour into crust(s).  Chill until set.
