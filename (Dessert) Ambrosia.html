Dessert: Ambrosia

I think we have had Ambrosia for every Thanksgiving, Christmas and Easter that I can remember.  I love it and I was so happy when I saw that Mom had written the recipe down for us.  It took her a long time to make because each orange section has to be removed individually.  It was truly a labor of love when she made it.  She would put it in those big ground glass jars and keep it in the fridge.  I love it for breakfast, too.  She also served it with pound cake sometimes.

She always kept a small bowl of grated coconut beside the ambrosia bowl.  She was a purist!!  It was to be added as a topping, not in her ambrosia!   


Ambrosia


2 bags Florida Juice Oranges (not navel)

1 small can Crushed Pineapple (Dole)

1 cup  sugar

 

No directions were included just ingredients!  She knew we would know what to do.  : )
