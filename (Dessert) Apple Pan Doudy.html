Dessert: Apple Pan Doudy

This is credited to Delores Smith and given to Mom by Bobbe Batteiger, Uncle Bob's wife.  It is dated 1956, Miami.


Apple Pan Doudy


apples   6 med.                         flour   1 C

sugar   1/2 c                             baking powder   1 tsp

cinnamon   1/2 tsp                    salt 1/2 tsp

water   1/4 cup                         shortening 1/4 c

butter 1 tbsp                            milk 1/2 c


     Core apples and cut in 1" slices.  Arrange in bottom of greased 9" pan.  Combine sugar and cinnamon, sprinkle over top.  Add water, dot with butter.  Cut in shortening into flour until mixture is like meal.  Add milk and mix to a soft dough.  Spoon this over apples to within 1" of edge.  Bake in hot oven (400 degrees) about 40 minutes until apples are tender.  Serve warm with whipped or plain cream.  


Serves 6 --
