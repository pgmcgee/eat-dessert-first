Dessert: Aunt Jenny's Cookies

Aunt Jenny was Peg Batteiger's sister; Dad's maternal aunt


Aunt Jenny's Cookies


1 - 9 oz. crushed pineapple (drain well)

1 c. chopped dates

1/2 c. shortening - Crisco

1 c. light brown sugar

1 egg

1` tsp. vanilla

2 cups flour

1 tsp. b. p.

1/4 tsp. salt

1/2 c. chopped nuts


     Grease cookie sheet.  Bake 12 - 15 min. - 400 degrees
