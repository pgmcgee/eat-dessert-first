Dessert: Bishops Cake

This is from Roxanne, Mom and Dad's next door neighbor at Lake Lanier.


Bishops Cake


1 box yellow cake mix

1 8 oz. Cool Whip

Frosting

  8 oz. cream cheese

  1 box vanilla instant pudding

  1 large can crushed pineapple - undrained


2 - 8" round pans


1.  Follow instructions on cake mix.  Bake in 2 - 8" round pans.  Let cool.

2.  Mix all of the frosting ingredients together in bowl.


Place frosting mixture in the middle and top.


Add Cool Whip to top of frosing.


You can add Cool Whip to sides if you like


Keep in frig. until ready to serve.
