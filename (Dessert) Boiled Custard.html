Dessert: Boiled Custard

This recipe is from the 1940's.  Mom always made it for us when we were sick.  It is incomplete but submitted here for memory sake!!


Boiled Custard


2 1/2 cups milk (sweet) approx.


Put on high burner until milk skims.


2 eggs, beaten with beater 

about 3/4 cup sugar; put in eggs.


As soon as skim comes on milk
