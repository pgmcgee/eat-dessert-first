Dessert: Brown Butter Icing

Mom recorded this recipe in 1960.  It was given to her by her beloved aunt, Polly.  It's from South Carolina.

 

Mae Wright's Brown Butter Icing

 

1 stick butter

1 box powdered sugar

 

Cream together in electric mixer.  Thin with canned cream and beat until creamy.

 

Add 1 tsp. vanilla and spread on layers.
