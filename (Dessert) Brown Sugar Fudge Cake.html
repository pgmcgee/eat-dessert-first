Dessert: Brown Sugar Fudge Cake

This recipe is in Nana's handwriting and on the back of the front page of a Christmas card.  Looks to be early 60's judging from the design of the card.


Brown Sugar Fudge Cake


2 1/4 cups cake flour

2      cups light brown sugar - 1 pk-

1  teaspoon soda

1/2   cup shortening - (1 Stick)

1/2   cup Buttermilk or sour milk (or add 2 Tablesppons of vinegar to s. milk)

1  teaspoon vanilla

3  eggs

2 ounces unsweetened chocolate, melted - (2 squares)


Sift flour, sugar, soda and salt into mixing bowl.  Add shortening, 2/3 of the buttermilk and vanilla.  Beat 250 strokes or 2 minutes in mixer at medium speed.  Add remaining milk, eggs and chocolate.  Beat as for first stage.  Pour batter into 3 greased and floured 8-in. round cake pans.  Bake at 350 for 30 to 35 minutes - cool.
