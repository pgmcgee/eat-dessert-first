Easy Double Chocolate Chip Brownies

 

2  c. (12 oz.) Toll House Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels, divided

1/2 c. (1 stick) butter or margarine, cut in pieces

3 eggs

1 1/4 c. flour

1 c. sugar

1/4 tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. vanilla

1/2 c. chopped nuts - optional


Melt 1 c. morsels and butter in large pan over low heat; stir until smooth.  Remove from heat.


Add eggs; stir well.


Add flour, sugar, baking soda, and vanilla.  Stir well.


Stir in remaining chocolate chips (1 c.) and nuts.  


Spread into greased 13" x 9" pan.  Bake at 350 for 18 - 22 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clear.  Cool completely.
