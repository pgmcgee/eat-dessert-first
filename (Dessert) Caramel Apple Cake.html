Dessert: Caramel Apple Cake

Caramel Apple Cake with Caramel Topping

 

2 1/2 c. sugar

3 eggs

1 1/2 c. vegetable oil

3 c. flour, all purpose

2 tsp. vanilla

1 c. chopped walnuts

2 1/2 c. diced apples, canned or fresh

 

Preheat oven to 350.  Cream sugar, eggs and oil.  Add flour; mix together until well blended.  Add vanilla, nuts and apples.  Spread into a lightly greased and floured 13 x 9" baking dish.  Bake for 45 - 60 minutes.  Cake is done when toothpick comes out clean.  Punch hles with knife and pour topping over.

 

Carmel Topping

 

3 sticks butter

2 c. brown sugar

1/4 c. milk

 

Heat all ingredients together over medium heat.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Let boil about 2 minutes.  Pour over warm cake.
