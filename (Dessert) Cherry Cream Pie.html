Dessert: Cherry Cream Pie

This recipe is from Shirley Dodge, Apex Optical, Orlando, Florida.  Dad was the salesman for Bob and Shirley Dodge for many years.  

 

Cherry Cream Pie

 

4 oz. softened cream cheese

1/2 c. powdered sugar

1 tsp. vanilla

1/2 tsp. cinnamon

 

Beat all above together.

 

Whip 1/2 pt. heavy cream and stir together with above cream mixture.  Pour and spread into bottom of a baked pie shell or graham cracker crust.  Cover with 1 can cherry pie filling.  

 

Refrigerate 3 to 4 hours before serving.   Can substitute a 9 oz. carton of Cool Whip for the cream but reduce amount of powdered sugar as the Cool Whip is already sweetened.
