Dessert: Chocolate Icing

Mom took this recipe to the beach.

 

 

Lanita's Chocolate Icing

 

 

1 stick margarine

4 Tbsp. cocoa

6 Tbsp. milk

1 box powdered sugar

1 tsp. vanilla

1 c. chopped nuts (optional)

 

Start icing 5 minutes before cake is done.  In pan, mix margarine, cocoa and milk.  Melt and bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and add powdered sugar, vanilla and pecans.  Beat well and spread on cake while hot.  Frost in pan.
