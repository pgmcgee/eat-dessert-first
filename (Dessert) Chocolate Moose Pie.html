Chocolate Moose Pie


1   pkg. Baker's German Sweet Choc or 8 oz. Hershey Bar w/ Almonds

1/3 c. milk

2 Tbsp. sugar

1-3 oz. pkg. cream ch., softened

1-8 oz. whipped topping, thawed

1-8" Graham cracker crust


Melt choc and 2 tbsp. milk.  Blend till choc melted.  Beat sugar into cream cheese.  Add remaining milk and choc mixtured & beat till smooth.  Fold choc mixture into whipped topping, blending till smooth.  Spoon into crust.  Freeze till firm (4 hrs.).
