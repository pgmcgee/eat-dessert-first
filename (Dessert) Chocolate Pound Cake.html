Dessert: Chocolate Pound Cake

This is Nana's recipe.  I actually have the original (or one of them) and Mom made some clarifications on it for me.  The paper is rather yellow and stained.  The recipe says it is also Mitt Stevenson's, Loreda's and Anna Bell's recipe.  I guess it was tried and true.  

 

I can always remember Nana and Mom making this for us.  It was definitely a favorite of mine and I am sure I can speak for all of us.  

 

 

Chocolate Pound Cake

1 cup butter

1/2 cup crisco 

3 cups sugar

5 eggs

1 Tsb vanilla    

3 cups plain flour

1/2 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 cup cocoa

1 1/4 cup milk

 

Cream together butter and crisco, add sugar, add eggs one at a time, vanilla, and then add all dry ingredients with milk.

 

Bake in a bundt pan at 325 for 1hour 15 minutes.
