Dessert: Chocolate Sheet Cake

This recipe is from South Carolina.  It is credited to Frank Blair and served at Ruby's birthday party on March 2, 1970.  I'm sure at least some of us were there!


Frank Blair's Sheet Chocolate Cake


2 cups plain flour

2 cups sugar

1 stick butter

1/2 cup Crisco

1 cup water

3 1/2 Tbsp. cocoa

1/2 cup buttermilk

2 unbeaten eggs

1 tsp. vanilla

1 tsp. soda

1/2 tsp. salt


Place flour and sugar in bowl and mix.


Combine butter, Crisco, water, and cocoa in sauce pan and heat to boiling, pour hot mixture over flour and sugar.  Mix well.


Add remaining ingredients and mix.


Pour into greased 12" x 15" baking pan.  Bake 20 minutes at 400.


Also makes a two layer cake.


Chocolate Icing


1 stick butter 

3 1/2 Tbsp. cocoa

1/2 c. buttermilk

1 lb. confectioners sugar

1 tsp. vanilla

nuts 


Mix and boil until slightly thick, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and add 1 pound confectioners sugar, vanilla and nuts.  Mix well and spread on cake in the pan.
