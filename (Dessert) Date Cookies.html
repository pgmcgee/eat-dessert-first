Dessert: Date Cookies

From Anne Wilson


Date Cookies


Mix in given order:


2 eggs

2/3 c. butter

1 c. white sugar

1 lb. dates, chopped

1 c. nuts

1 tsp. cloves

1 tsp. cinnamon

4 Tbsp. hot water

1 tsp. soda, dissolved in hot water

2 1/2 c. flour
