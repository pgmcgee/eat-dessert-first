Dessert: Easy White Fudge Frosting

This is Sarah Anderson's recipe, Coral Gables, FL

 

Easy White Fudge Frosting

 

1/2 c. butter

1 c. granulated sugar

1/4 c. milk

1 3/4 to 2 c. 10X sugar

1 tsp. vanilla

 

Melt butter in pan.  Remove from heat.

 

Add granulated sugar and milk, stirring until well blended.   Bring to boiling and stir.  Remove from heat.  Cool slightly.


Gradually beat 1 3/4 c. 10X sugar into warm mixture, beating well after each addition, until frosting is thick enough to spread.  Add more 10X if needed.


Add vanilla.  


Makes a small amount of frosting.
