Dessert: Fluffy mocha Frosting

 Fluffy Mocha Frosting


1/3 c. margarine

4    c. sifted conf. sugar

1/4 c. cocoa

1/4 tsp. salt

1/3 c. strong coffee

1   tsp. vanilla


Cream butter.  Sift together conf. sugar, cocoa and salt.  Add part of sugar mixture gradually to butter, blending after each addition.  Add remaining sugar mixture alternately with coffee, using more than 1/3 cup if needed to have frosting of right consistency to spread.  Beat after each addition until smooth.  Stir in vanilla and frost cake.
