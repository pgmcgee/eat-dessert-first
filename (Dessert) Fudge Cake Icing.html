Dessert: Fudge Cake Icing

This is from Mom's home ec classes at University of Miami, around 1949 - 1950.

 

Fudge Cake Icing

 

2 T. butter

4 T. canned milk

2 c. powdered sugar

1/4 c. cocoa

 

Cook in double boiler until melted.  Melt butter, add cocoa, add milk, then sugar.  Spread over cake.
