Dessert: Fudge Cake

This is a very old recipe from Nana's collection.  It is in her sister, Mabel's, handwriting.  Nana credits the recipe to Billy Clayton.

 

Fudge Cake

 

1/2 c. butter

1 c. sugar

3/4 c. flour

1/2 t. baking powder

1/2 c. cocoa

8 eggs

1 c. walnuts

1 t. vanilla

 

Cream butter and sugar well, add flour, baking powder, cocoa and vanilla.  Beat eggs thoroughly with rotary beater and add to mixture; mix well.  Then add the nuts.

 

Bake in a greased pan in a moderate oven.
