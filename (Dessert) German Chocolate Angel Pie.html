Dessert: German Chocolate Angel Pie

This is one long recipe!!  It's from Sue Lexow so I know Mom cherished it.  


German Chocolate Angel Pie


Meringue Shell


     Combine 2 egg whites, 1/8 tsp. salt, 1/8 tsp. cream of tartar in mixing bowl.  Beat until foamy.  Add 1/2 cup sugar. 2 tbsp. at a time, beating after each until sugar is blended.  Continue beating until mixture stands in very stiff peaks.  Fold in  1/2 cup finely chopped pecans and 1/2 tsp. vanilla.  Spoon intonlightly greased 8" pie pan; make a nest-like shell, building sides up 1/2" above edge of pan.  Bake in slow (300 degrees) oven for 50 - 55 minutes.  Cool to room temp  (Meringue Shell or entire pie may be made the day before using)


Chocolate Angel Filling


     Place 1 pkg. (1/4 lb.) Baker's German Sweet Chocolate and 3 tbsp. water in a saucepan over low heat.  Stir until chocolate is melted.  Cool until thickened.  

     Add 1 tsp. vanilla.  Whip 1 c. whipping cream.  Fold chocolate mixture into whipped cream.  Pile into meringue shell.  Chill 2 hrs. before serving.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
