Dessert: Ice Box Cookies

This is from Mrs. Roy Nowlin who lived across the street (Cantoria Ave., Coral Gables, FL) from Nana and Big Daddy.  This would have been the late 1940's or early '50's; most likely just before Mom and Dad were married.


Ice Box Cookies


2 c. brown sugar

2 eggs

1 c. shortening

3 1/2 c. flour - with heaping tsp. cinnamon

1/2 T. soda

1/2 T. cream of tartar   (this is capital T as in Tablespoon)

nuts

flavoring - 1 tsp.
salt


Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, then add flour, soda, c. of t., and salt.  


Roll into 2 rolls and put in refrigerator.  


Bake about 375 - 400 degrees.
