Dessert: Lanita's Fruit Dip

This recipe was given to me by a "Navy" friend. Mom loved it (and she loved Lanita) and took it to one of her happy hours in the Villages. When I arrived the next day she had saved some for us to share. She was so happy because she had our "dinner" ready!!! : ) 

 

Lanita's Dip for Fruit

 

8 oz pkg. soft cream cheese

4 oz sour cream

1/2 cup white sugar

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/2 tsp cinnamon

 

Stir all ingredients together and serve with your favorite fruit.
