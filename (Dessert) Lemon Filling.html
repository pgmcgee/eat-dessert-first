Dessert: Lemon Filling

This is Mom's grandmother's recipe.  It is in Aunt Polly's handwriting and Nana dated it 1958.  


Lemon Filling for Cake


1 cup water

3 eggs - beaten

1 1/2 cups sugar

2 rounding tablespoon of flour or 2 level tablespoon of cornstarch

1/4 stick of butter or margarine

1/2 cup of water in lemon juice

2 lemons - grate rind of both - add to juice - mix and cook in double boiler until thick and smooth
