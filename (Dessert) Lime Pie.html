Dessert: Lime Pie

These two recipes are written on the back of a sheet of memo paper from the desk of L. D. Gore (Big Daddy).  It was the note paper he used at the Birmingham Seafoof Co. he owned in the 30's and 40's before they moved to Coral Gables.


Lucy's Lime Pie


1 can condensed milk

6 egg whites

4 egg yolks

1/2 c. sugar

1/2 c. lime juice


Use about 1/3 of egg whites in pie filling.  


That's all she wrote for directions for that version!!!  Another version follows below.

 

Magic Lime Pie (8" pie)


1 can condensed milk

1/2 c. lime juice

1 tsp. grated lime rind

1 c. heavy cream, whipped


Combine c. milk, lime juice and rind.  Stir until well blended and thickened.  Fold in half of whipped cream.  Spread remaining whip cream on top.  Chill.
