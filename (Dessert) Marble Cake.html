Dessert: Marble Cake

This recipe is from Kathy Mummery's collection, Coral Gables, FL.  

 

Marquita's Marble Cake

 

1 pkg. Duncan Hines Yellow Cake Mix

4 eggs

2/3 cup sour cream

1 pkg. Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix

1/2 cup oil

1/3 cup water

 

Put all ingredients in bowl and beat until mixed.  Then, beat 4 minutes at high speed.  Put 2/3 batter in pan.  Add small can of Hershey's syrup to remaining 1/3 mix.  Add to remaining batter in pan and swirl.  

 

Bake at 350 for 55 minutes to 1 hour.  Let stand 15 minutes before turning it out.

 

Variations:

 

Make it a lemon cake by substituting lemon cake mix and lemon pudding.

 

Make it a chocolate cake by substituting chocolate cake mix and chocolate and leaving out the Hershey's syrup.
