Dessert: Meringue

From the University of Miami collection.

 

Meringue

 

2 egg whites

2 tbsp. confectioner's sugar

1/8 tsp. cream of tartar

pinch of salt

1/2 tsp. lemon or vanilla

 

Beat egg whites until foamy.  Add cram of tartar and salt.  Beat until stiff, but not dry.  Beat in sugar gradually.  Add flavoring.  

 

Place on pie, do not smooth.  


Bake very slowly or at least 15 minutes to 300 to 325.
