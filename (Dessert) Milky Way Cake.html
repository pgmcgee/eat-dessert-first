Dessert: Milky Way Cake

From Nana!  Note the description of the size of the candy bars!


Milky Way Cake


4 (10 cent) Milky Way - or 8 (5 cent) small 

2 sticks butter (oleo)  Melt 1 st. of butter + Milky Way

2 cups sugar

2 1/2 cups flour (plain)

1/2 teas. soda

1 1/2 cup Buttermilk

1 cup pecans

4 eggs


Bake 325 - 1 1/2 hours


Cream butter add sugar or eggs one at the time - Milky Way + butter then flour + Buttermilk - Bake in tube pan.
