Dessert: Mom's Baked Custard

This was the ultimate comfort food!  We always had it when we were sick.  It tasted soooo good!!  Mom revised it from Nana's original recipe by tweaking the ingredients to make a larger portion.  Mom's recipe is directly below.  Nana's ingredients follow for comparison.

 

Mom's Baked Custard

September 21, 1996 

 

5 eggs (medium)

1 c. sugar

pinch salt

 

Beat the three ingredients together.

 

4 c. skim milk

1/2 tsp. vanilla

 

Add milk and vanillas to egg mixture and beat.

 

Put water in pan with baking cups Fill baking cups (about 10) and bake at 350 about 35 minutes.   

 

 

Nana's Original Baked Custard (Ingredients only, cooking instructions the same)

 

2 eggs - beaten

1/2 c. sugar

1/8 tsp. salt

2 c. milk

1/2 tsp. vanilla
