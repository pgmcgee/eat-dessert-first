Dessert: Oma's Apple Pie

8-10 medium golden delicious apples*
3/4 cup sugar
2 T flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
dash salt
2 T butter, cut up
Double pie crust

*can add ~ 1 T lemon juice if apples aren't tart enough

Peel, core and slice apples; add lemon juice if needed.? Combine apples with spice mixture (sugar through salt) and pour into pie shell.? Top with dots of butter.? Cover with second pie crust.

Bake at 400 for 50 minutes.
