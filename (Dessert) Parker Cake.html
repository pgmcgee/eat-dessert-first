Dessert: Parker Cake

My friend Jane Emerson brought this cake to us when Dad died. Parker just loved it. Even the Emerson family have changed the name to Parker Cake! 

 

Parker Cake

 

1 yellow cake mix

1 (5.9 oz) pkg instant chocolate pudding (make sure you use instant)

4 eggs, beaten

2/3 cup vegetable oil

2/3 cup sugar

1/3 cup water

1 8 oz container sour cream

1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

 

Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour bundt pan. Mix cake mix, pudding mix, oil, sugar, eggs and water. Gently fold in sour cream and chocolate chips. Pour into pan. Bake 55 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
