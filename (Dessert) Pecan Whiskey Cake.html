Dessert: Pecan Whiskey Cake

This is undated and from a newspaper.

 

Pecan Whiskey Cake

 

2 cups flour

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 cup butter, softened

1 cup sugar

4 eggs, beaten

1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmet

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup blended whiskey

1 cup raisins, chopped

2 cups pecans, coarsely chopped

 

In bowl, toss flour with baking powder to combine; set aside.  Cream butter and sugar until fluffy; beat in eggs one at a time; add nutmeg and salt.  Add flour mixture alternately with whiskey, reserving 1/4 cup of the flour mixture.  Blend well after each addition.  Combine raisins and pecans; toss with reserved flour and fold into batter.  Grease generously and lightly flour an 8x8" baking pan.  Pour in batter and bake in moderately slow (325) oven about 1 hour, until cake is firm to the touch.  Cool in pan, serve in squares.  Makes 9 servings.
