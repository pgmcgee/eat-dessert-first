Dessert: Pound Cake (2)

This is Mary Dickerson's cake.  Mom commented:  "Good - 8-3-69"

 

Mary Dickerson's Pound Cake

 

2 1/2 c. plain flour

2 c. sugar

5 eggs

1 tsp. baking powder

1 c. butter

3/4 c. milk

1 tsp. lemon flavoring

1 tsp. vanilla

 

Cream butter and sugar.  Beat egg yolks and whites separately.  

 

Add yolks to butter and sugar.  Then, add flour, baking powder alternately with milk.  Add flavorings.

 

Put in tube pan.

 

Cook at 325 for 1 hour without opening oven.
