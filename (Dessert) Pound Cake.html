Dessert: Pound Cake

This is Mary Dickerson's recipe.  Mom really liked it and called it the "dump" cake because you dumped in all the ingredients at once.  Nana used to comment that it's not a "real" pound cake!!


NOTE:  Another version of this recipe is EXACTLY the same except you add 2 cups plain flour (plus the 1 cup self-rising flour called for in the recipe below).  Also see "Dump" Pound Cake recipe.  It's very similar.

 

Mary Dickerson's Pound Cake 

 

1 stick margarine at room temperature

5 large eggs

1 cup Crisco

1 cup whole milk

2 1/2 cups sugar

1 cup self-rising flour

1 teaspoon vanilla

2 or more teaspoons Lemon flavoring

 

Place all ingredients in mixer bowl.  Beat on cream setting for 10 minutes.  

 

Pour into greased and floured tube pan.  Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes at 350.

 

Let cool in pan long enough so it won't stick to pan (about 10 to 15 minutes)
