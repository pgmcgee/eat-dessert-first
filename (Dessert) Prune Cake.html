Dessert: Prune Cake

This recipe was typed by Etta Rosson.  Nana commented "so good" and dated it April 5, 1962.  She said it was served at the Boarding House.  It is my understanding the Jr. baby prunes are no longer available.  A puree prune is still made by Beechnut.  I think I remember someone used plums and thought it was delicious.  I've heard this recipe titled "spice" cake for those not fond of prunes!! 

 

Etta's Prune Cake

 

2 cups sugar

2 cups self-rising flour

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1 teaspoon nutmeg

1 teaspoon allspice

 

Sift all above together.

 

3 eggs

1 cup Wesson oil

1 jar Junior size baby prunes

1 cup nuts

 

Beat eggs, oil and prunes.  Add dry ingredients and nuts.

 

Bake in tube pan at 350 for 45 - 60 minutes.  

 

Sprinkle top with powdered sugar.
