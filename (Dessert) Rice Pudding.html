Dessert: Rice Pudding

This is Nana's recipe. There is a variation for each of these.  Guess we'll just have to try them and see what works!! You can use the same basic ingredients for bread pudding; recipe below.

 

Nana's Rice Pudding


2 eggs

1/2 cup sugar

1 cup milk

2 tbsp. butter

1 tbsp. flour - scant

rice; cooked

vanilla


Beat eggs.  Add flour, sugar, butter and rice.  Stir and add milk gradually.  


Bake at 325 til it shakes in a little circle in the middle.  


Make meringue with 2 egg whites and 4 tbsp. sugar.  Bake at 350 until golden.

 

Rice Pudding

 

1 tbsp. flour

3 eggs

scant cup of sugar

2 c. milk

1/4 stick butter

1 1/2 cups rice

1 tsp. vanilla


Beat egg yolks.  Add sugar and flour.  Then add milk and rice alternately.  Add vanilla.  Bake at 300 - 350 until nearly firm.  Then add meringue made from:


3 egg whites

6 tbsp. sugar


Bake at 350 - 400 until light brown. 


Nana's Bread Pudding 


Same as rice pudding except toast 2 or 3 slices of bread (with butter).  Add raisins but no meringue. 



Bread Pudding


Same as Rice Pudding above (second recipe) - toast and butter bread unit crunchy (3 or 4 slices).  Add raisins; don't put meringue.  Add whole eggs.  Then, she adds a milk sauce:


1 c. milk

1 tbsp. butter

scant tbsp. flour

scant cup sugar


Let get hot, but not boiling.  Add a little nutmeg.
