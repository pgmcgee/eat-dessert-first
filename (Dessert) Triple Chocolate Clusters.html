Dessert: Triple Chocolate Clusters

This is compliments of Laurie.  She says it's delicious!! 

 

Triple Chocolate Clusters

 

2-4 oz. white chocolate bars (Ghirardelli)  

1 cup (6 oz.) milk chocolate morsels

1 cup (6 oz.) semisweet chocolate morsels

1 1/2 cups chopped pecans

1 1/2 cups broken pretzels


Melt first 3 ingredients in a heavy saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly.  Stir in pecans and pretzels.  Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls onto lightly greased wax paper.  Chill in refrigerator at least one hour.  


Store in airtight container in refrigerator up to one month.


Yield:  about 3 dozen
