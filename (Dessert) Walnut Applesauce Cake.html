Dessert: Walnut Applesauce Cake

This recipe was clipped by Mom from a magazine.  My guess would be circa 1955.

 

Walnut Applesauce Cake

 

Use 2 8" layer pans

 

1 pkg. white cake mix

1/4 tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. cinnamon

1/4 tsp. nutmeg

1/2 tsp. powdered cloves

1 cup canned applesauce

1 cup finely chopped walnuts

3/4 cup seedless raisins

 

Grease, then line bottoms of layer pans with waxed paper.  Turn mix into bowl; add soda and next 3 ingredients; then add applesauce in place of 1/2 cup liquid called for in first beating.  

 

Now complete as directed; then stir in nuts and raisins.  

 

Turn into pans.  Bake 25 minutes at 375 or until done.  

 

Cool in pans on racks 10 minutes; remove, peel off paper, cool.

 

Frost with Orange Cream Cheese:

 

Blend two 3 oz. pkgs. cream cheese, 2 tbsp. orange juice.  Stir in 4 1/2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar gradually; add dash salt and 1 tsp. grated orange rind.  Blend well.  Sprinkle orange rind over top of frosting.
