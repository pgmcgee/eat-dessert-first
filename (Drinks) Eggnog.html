Drinks: Eggnog

Mom recorded Ann Batteiger's eggnog during the Christmas holiday, 2006.  Ann says it from the Orland Sentinel "a long time ago"!  Be sure to heed the warning!!

 

Ann's (Killer) Eggnog

 

1 dozen eggs, separated

2 c. sugar

1 qt. rum, Ronrico white works

1 qt. milk, non-fat is fine

1 qt. half-and-half, fat free or cream

Nutmeg

 

Whip egg whites until stiff, pouring in 1 cup of the sugar.  Whip yolks until frothy, pouring in remaining sugar.  Gently fold yolks and whites together into large punch bowl.  Pour in all of the rum and stir.

 

Pour in milk and half-and-half. DO NOT STIR.  Top with nutmeg.  Chill or float a few ice cubes in bowl.  


Dip and serve!


WARNING:  People who are dour and glum should not drink more than one cup unless they are prepared to spontaneously break out with feelings of good cheer.
