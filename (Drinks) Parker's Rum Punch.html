Parker's Rum Punch 

This is the closest recipe we have found to the delicious ones we had at Scrub Island in the BVI. Cheers!

1 part guava nectar
1 part pineapple juice
1 part orange juice
1 part rum 
1/4 part grenadine

Mix all ingredients together in a pitcher and chill for 2 hours. Serve over ice with a sprinkle of nutmeg. Yum!
