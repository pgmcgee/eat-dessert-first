Pepin's Sangria

 

1 cups ice, crushed

1/4 cup sugar

3 shakes cinnamon

1 lime wedge

1 lemon wedge

1 orange slice

1/2 cup brandy

1/2 cup cherry brandy

1/2 cup triple sec

1 liter Cella Lambrusco

14 oz. (1/2 bottle) tonic water

 

Squeeze the lime, lemon and orange and drop in.  Mix all ingredients in pitcher, including ice.
