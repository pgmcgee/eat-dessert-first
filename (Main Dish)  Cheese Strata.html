Main Dish: Cheese Strata

This is from Aggie Spies, Ann Batteiger's mother, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Cheese Strata

 

12 slices white bread

3/4 lb. sliced American cheese

1-10 oz. pkg. frozen broccoli or spinach

2 cups cooked, diced ham or chicken

 

Mix:  6 slightly beaten eggs, 3 1/2 cups milk, 2 T. grated onion, 1/2 tsp. salt, 3/4 tsp. dry mustard and 1/2 c. dry sherry.

 

Cut bread with donut cutter in 9x13" pan.  Layer extra pieces of bread in bottom.  Ham, chicken, broccoli, cheese in any order.  

 

Top with 12 "bread donuts" or "holes".  Pour egg mixture over all.  Refrigerate 6 hours or overnight.  

 

Bake at 325 for 55 minutes.  Last 5 minutes, grate cheese over top and sprinkle with paprika.  Remove from oven and let set 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
