Main Dish: Baked Ziti

This is the Baked Ziti Laurie makes; dated 1994

 

La's Baked Ziti

 

1- 6 oz. pkg. ziti

1 lb. mild sausage

1/2 lb. ground beef

1/2 c. chopped onion

3 c. (24 oz.) spaghetti sauce

1 - 6 oz. pkg. mozzarella cheese (sliced) 

1/4 (or 1/2) c. grated Parmesan cheese

 

Cook ziti.  Brown meats in skillet with onion.  Spread 1/2 ziti in 9" x 13" pan.  Put slices of mozzarella over ziti and cover with 1/2 spaghetti sauce.  

 

Layer rest of ziti and spaghetti sauce.  

 

Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 15 minutes.  Uncover and add rest of mozzarella and parmesan cheese.  Bake for 15 more minutes.
