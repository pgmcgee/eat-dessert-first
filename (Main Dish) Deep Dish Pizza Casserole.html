Main Dish: Deep Dish Pizza Casserole

Mom copied this recipe in July, 2005 while at the Batteiger's, Jacksonville

 

Deep Dish Pizza Casserole

 

1 lb. ground round

1 - 15 oz. can Chunky Italian style tomato sauce

1 - 10 oz. can refrigrated pizza crust dough

6 - 1 oz. slices mozzarella cheese, divided

 

Cook meat until crumbly.  Drain and return to skillet.  Add tomato sauce and cook until heated.  

 

While meat cooks, spray 9 x 13" dish.  Unroll pizza crust dough and press on bottom and halfway up side.  Put 3 slices cheese on bottom.  Top with meat mixture.  Bake at 425 for 12 minutes.  Top with cheese and bake 5 minutes more.
