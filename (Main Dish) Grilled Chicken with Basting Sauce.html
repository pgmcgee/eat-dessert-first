Main Dish: Grilled Chicken with Basting Sauce

Grilled Chicken with Basting Sauce

 

1/4 cup butter

2 Tbsp red wine vinegar

2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

1 Tbsp sugar

1 1/2 tsp lemon juice

1 garlic clove, chopped

1/2 tsp dried crushed red pepper

1/2 tsp coarsely ground black pepper

8 bone-in chicken breasts

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper


Combine first 8 ingredients and cook over medium-low heat,stirring occasionally, until butter 0melts. Set aside.

Sprinkle chicken evenly with salt and pepper.

Grill chicken for about 40-45 minutes or until done, turning occasionally and basting with sauce the last 10 minutes.


We made this last night (3-6-2008).  Easy and delicious.  We also grilled some asparagus with it and made sweet potato fries.
