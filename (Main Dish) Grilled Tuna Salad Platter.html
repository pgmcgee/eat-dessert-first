Main Dish: Grilled Tuna Salad Platter

This is a new recipe.  We tried it last night and it passed the test!  

 

 

Grilled Tuna Salad Platter

 

1 lb. fresh tuna steaks, cut 1 inch thick

8 oz. green beans, trimmed

1 lb. small red potatoes

1 medium red onion, cut in wedges

8 grape tomatoes

2 hard cooked eggs

8 pitted kalamata olives 

1 head Boston or Bibb lettuce

3 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

Parmesan cheese, grated

Farmer Boy Restaurant Greek Dressing

 

Place fish in plastic bag or shallow dish.  Drizzle with 1/3 cup Greek dressing.  Cover and marinate in refrigerator for 30 minutes.  


Peel a strip around the center of each potato; place potatoes in large saucepan and cover with water.  Bring to boil over high heat; reduce heat and simmer, covered for 10 minutes.  Add green beans; simmer, covered for 5 more minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Drain.

 

Drain tuna and reserve marinade.  Grill over medium heat 8 to 12 minutes or until tuna flakes easily.  Brush with marinade halfway through cooking.  Place onion, potatoes and green beans on grill halfway through cooking time.  Discard remaining marinade.


Flake tuna into pieces.  Cut eggs in halves.  Arrange fish, potatoes, green beans, tomatoes, eggs, and olives on platter.  Drizzle with Greek dressing.  Line individual plates with lettuce and drizzle with dressing before serving.  


You can garnish the platter with fresh orange slices to add color a fruit.  


Substitute vinaigrette dressing:


1/2 cup olive oil

1/3 cup white wine vinegar

2 T. drained capers

2 t. sugar

2 t. Dijon mustard

1/2 t. salt

1/2 t. black pepper


Combine all above in screw-top jar.  Cover and shake well.  Reserve 1/3 up to use as marinade.
