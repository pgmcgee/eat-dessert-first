Main Dish: Marie's Chicken Pie

This recipe if famous in our family.  It is from Marie Zachary during the St. Pete days. 

 

Marie's Chicken Pie


Boil chicken, bone.  Grease dish. 


Add in:

1 can mushroom soup

2 1/2 cups stock

Layer chicken, 1 can of carrots,1 can of peas.


Add 1 T flour to thicken stock and soup.


Topping:

1 cup self-rising flour

1 T. baking powder

1 cup milk

1 stick margarine, melted

 

Bake at 425 for 30-35 minutes.
