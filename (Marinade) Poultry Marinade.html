Marinade: Poultry Marinade

This is in Cathy's writing.  She comments that it can be used on turkey or chicken.

 

Poultry Marinade

 

1 part vegetable oil (Wesson oil)

1 part soy sauce

2 parts white wine

 

Marinate up to 72 hours for a large turkey breast; 12 - 24 hours, minimum.  

 

Cook or grill as desired.
