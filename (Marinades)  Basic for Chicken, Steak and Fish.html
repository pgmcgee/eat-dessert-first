Marinades: Basic for Chicken, Steak and Fish

This was a recipe Mom used at Perdido.  It was included in her notebook dedicated to the recipes she took to the beach.

 

 

Marinade for Chicken, Steak and Fish

from Home Show, April, 1989

 

 

1 c. Tamari Sauce

1 or 2 tsp. olive oil

2 - 3  cloves garlic

1 - 2 tsp. Dijon Mustard

 

Mix above together.  

 

Marinate chicken  and steak overnight.

Marinate fish about 1/2 hour.
