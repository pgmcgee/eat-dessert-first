Meat: Slow Cooker Beef

Mom copied this from Ann's Slow Cooker book.  It is dated 7-6-05.

 

Burgundy Beef

 

1 boneless chuck roast, 2 1/4 to 2 1/2 lbs.

1 can cream of mushroom soup, 10 3/4 oz.

1 can condensed creamy onion soup

1 c. sliced mushrooms and/or chopped onion, optional

1/4 - 1/2 c. Burgundy wine

 

Cut meat into 1" pieces.  Put in cooker.  

Stir in soups and wine.

Cover and cook on high 4 - 4.5 hours or on low 8 - 9 hours.

 

Serve with noodles and green beans.
