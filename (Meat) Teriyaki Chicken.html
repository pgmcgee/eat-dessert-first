Meat: Teriyaki Chicken

Mom called this one her own!  She and Dad did it together!  They must have enjoyed the meal and the evening.  She comments that they're going to cook it again!  Dated March 2, 1990

 

"My" Teriyaki Chicken 


2 Chicken Breasts


Pour Golden Dipt Teriyaki marinade over breasts.  (Then, add a little Kikkoman Teriyaki over that.)  

Then, pour some white wine over that!!  Let marinate for about 2 hours.  (Really more - I only had 2 hours!)  Turn frequently.


Microwave at 50% power for about 5 - 8 minutes - turning at 2 minute intervals.  


Then Skip puts it on the grill and finishes cooking.


It was delicious!!  Moist, no added seasoning (even salt) needed!!
