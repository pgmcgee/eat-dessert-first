Meats: King Ranch Chicken

This is from Dorothy McConnell, Aunt Sue's next door neighbor in Houston, TX.  Mom copied it on July 29, 1981.

 

King Ranch Chicken 

 

1 pkg. corn tortillas cut into bite size pieces

1 large fryer, boiled and boned, cut into pieces

1/2 lb. grated cheddar cheese

1 can Mushroom soup

1 can Rotel tomatoes with chilis

1 large onion, chopped

1 large bell pepper, chopped

garlic salt

1 can Cream of Chicken soup

1 tsp. chili powder


Layer tortillas, chicken, onion, pepper, cheese, garlic salt, soups, tomatoes.  Spread chili powder on top.


Bake at 325 for 1 hour.  


Good with guacamole salad and Ranch Style beans.


Serves 6 - 8
