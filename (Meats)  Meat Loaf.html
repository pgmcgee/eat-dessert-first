Meats: Meat Loaf

We never found an exact recipe for Mom's meatloaf.  I've made it several times and it's just not quite Mom's!!  Guess it never will be!!  The ingredients and some instructions are below.  There's a big "thank you" to the first one to perfect!!


Mom's Meatloaf


day old white bread, crumbled

ground chuck; Mom usually mixed 5 lbs. and made 2 or 3 loaves

eggs

milk

minced dried onions

salt 

pepper


Mix, with your bare hands, an appropriate amount of each to obtain a moist mixture that holds together when forming a loaf or loaves.  Put bacon on top to cover.  Bake in 350 oven for 30 minutes.  Add ketchup to top and bake another 30 minutes.
