Meats: Pot Roast

Mom got this one from Paula Deen's TV program.

 

Pot Roast

 

1 - 3 lb. boneless chuck roast

1 1/2 tsp. House Seasoning

1/4 c. vegetable oil

1 onion, thinly sliced

3 Bay leaves

3 or 4 bouillon cubes, crushed

2 cloves garlic, crushed

1 can cream of mushroom soup

1/4 to 1/2 cup Chardonnay wine

 

Sprinkle roast with seasoning and season well.  Brown roast in oil in hot skillet.  Place in crock pot.  Put all other ingredients on top of roast.  Add Chardonnay.  Cover with enough water to cover all ingredients.  Cook on low setting about 8 hours.
