Mom copied this recipe July, 2005 during her stay with Glenn and Ann Batteiger and the boys.

 

Blackberry Pork Chops

 

1 1/2 lbs. pork chops

2 tsp. Montreal Steak Seasoning (Grill Mate)

olive oil cooking spray

1/4 c. blackberry preserves

1 tsp. soy sauce

1 tsp. lemon juice

dash cinnamon

dash curry powder

1c. fresh blackberries (optional)

 

Pre-heat the grill.   Sprinkle both sides of chops with steak seasoning.  Spray both sides with Pam.  Place on grill.  Cover and grill 8 - 10 minutes.  Turn once.  Internal temp. should be 150 degrees.


Combine, in saucepan, preserves, soy sauce, lemon juice, cinnamon, and curry.  Bring just to boil while stirring.  Cover and remove from heat.


Ladle sauce over chops.  Garnish with blackberries.
