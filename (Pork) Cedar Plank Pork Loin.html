Pork: Cedar Plank Pork Loin

Mom copied this in July, 2005 while at the Batteiger's in Jacksonville.

 

Bo and Ann's Cedar Plank Pork Loin

 

1 cedar plank

1/2 c. BBQ sauce (Kraft or Bull's Eye)

1/4 c. teriyaki sauce

grated peel of medium orange

1 Tbsp. vegetable oil

1 boneless pork loin (2 - 21/2 lbs.)

 

Grill 1 hour or to internal temperature of 150 degrees basting with sauce during last 20 minutes.  

 

Save half of sauce for serving.
