Pork: Glazed Pork Chops

Mom copied this in July, 2005 while at the Batteiger's in Jacksonville.

 

Glazed Pork Chops

 

4 pork loin chips, 1/2" thick

cooking spray

1/8 tsp. ground sage

1/2 tsp. salt

1/4 tsp. pepper

1/4 c. minced onion

1/4 c. currant jelly

1 1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard

 

Spray skillet and heat until hot.  Cook chops sprinkled with sage, salt, pepper, 1 minute each side.  Reduce heat to medium and cook 4 - 5 minutes on each side until done.  Keep warm on platter.

 

Add onion to skillet and cook on medium 2 minutes, stirring often.  Reduce heat; add jelly and mustard and simmer 2 minutes until glaze is reduced to 1/4 c.  Spoon over chops.
