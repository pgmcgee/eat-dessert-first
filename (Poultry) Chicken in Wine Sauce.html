Poultry: Chicken in Wine Sauce

Chicken in Wine Sauce

 

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

6 oz. Swiss cheese slices

1 - 10oz. can cream of chicken soup

1/4 c. white wine - more if desired!

salt and pepper to taste

1 c. herb-flavored Pepperidge Farm stuffing, crushed

4 tbsp. (1/2 stick) butter, melted

 

Preheat oven to 350.  Place chicken in buttered casserole.  Layer cheese on top.  Mix soup, wine, salt and pepper; pour over cheese.  Sprinkle stuffing mix on top and drizzle with butter.  Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
