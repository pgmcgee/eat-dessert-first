Poultry: Honey Baked Ham

I don't know who to credit for this recipe.  It's in my handwriting and was in the journal Red set up for Mom at Lady Lake.  Perhaps one of Mom's visitors gave it to us...who knows??

 

Honey Baked Ham Recipe

 

1 cooked spiral ham half

1 c. sugar

1/4 t. cinnamon

1/4 t. nutmeg

1/4 t. cloves

1/8 t. paprika

dash of ginger

dash of allspice

 

Mix all ingredients in a small bowl.

 

Lay wax paper on counter.  Pour sugar mixture on wax paper and spread evenly.

 

Roll ham over sugar mixture to coat well.  Don't coat flat end of ham.

 

Turn ham on its flat side on a plate.  Caramelize with a blow torch.
