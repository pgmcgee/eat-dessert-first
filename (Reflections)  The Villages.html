Reflections: The Villages

Mom wrote this in one of her little notebooks.  It was written in purple ink, of course!!  It was titled, "The Villages".  it was dated in this fashion, "(2005 - I think!)"


"When we first looked at the V!!  I loved it from the very first time I came here!  I'm happier than I ever thought I could be without my Skip - my PaPa!!!  Thank you Brian & Laurie - and thank you - all our children and Grandchildren - I'm so blessed!

       Love & cherish all of you!"
