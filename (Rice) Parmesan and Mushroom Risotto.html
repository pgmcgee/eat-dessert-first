Rice: Parmesan and Mushroom Risotto

Here's the risotto recipe as Dad makes it.  Makes about 6 extra-large tasty servings.

1) Chop 1 onion then add olive oil to coat bottom of pot and cook onion over high heat until soft
2) In separate pan, saute baby bella mushrooms (1 pack) in butter
3) In another pot, heat 7-8 cups of Swanson chicken broth over high heat
4) Add 2 cups of risotto (arborrio) rice to onions and stir to coat rice with olive oil
5) Brown risotto rice a bit
6) Add one cup white wine to risotto/onion mix (optional)
7) Stir in chicken broth one ladle at a time, just enough to coat rice because you don't want a pot of liquid
     - Leave lid off to let the flavor intensify
8) Continue stirring rice and adding chicken broth as the rice absorbs the broth
     - Continue about 20 minutes until rice is almost done
9) When the risotto is almost done, add sauteed mushrooms, stir
10) Keep stirring until barely too wet to serve, and add about 1 1/2 cups shredded Parmesan
11) Turn off heat, let sit until perfect
12) Serve, eat, and enjoy!
