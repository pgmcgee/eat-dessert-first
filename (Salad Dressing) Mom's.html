Salad Dressing: Mom's

Mom calls this her salad dressing.  It calls for 1/6 cup of ketchup.  That's right!!  Knowing Mom, that was 1/2 the 1/3 cup measure!! 

 

"My" Salad Dressing

 

1/2 cup oil

1/4 cup wine vinegar

1/4 cup sugar or 1 tsp. Sweet n Low

1/6 cup ketchup

dash salt


Blend well and refrigerate.
