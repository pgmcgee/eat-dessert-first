Salad: Bean Salad

This is Nana's recipe.  It is good!  Later on, Mom switched to Reed's bean salad in a jar.  Dad loved bean salad.  We used to buy it in a HUGE jar at B.j.'s in Cumming.  He couldn't get enough of it and it was good for him!!


Bess' Bean Salad


1 can kidney beans (washed)

1 can waxed beans

1 can cut green beans (drain and mix)

1 large onion (cut thin)

1 carrot (cut thin)

Several piecesof celery

1/3 cup mazola 

2/3 cup vinegar

1/2 cup sugar

1 t black pepper

1 t salt


Mix all together in large bowl and marinate for 24 hours.  Stir about twice during this time.
