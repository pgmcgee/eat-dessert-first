Salad: Broccoli Slaw with Cranberries

Broccoli Slaw with Cranberries

 

1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

3/4 cup sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds

3 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 (12 or 16 oz.) package Broccoli Slaw mix

3 Gala apples, chopped

1 cup dried cranberries

 

Combine first four ingredients in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil and boil 1 minute (or until sugar dissolves).

 

Remove from heat and cool; whisk in oil.  

 

Combine slaw mix, apples and cranberries in a large bowl.  Pour vinaigrette over slaw and toss.


Cover and chill.

 

Yield:  8 cups
