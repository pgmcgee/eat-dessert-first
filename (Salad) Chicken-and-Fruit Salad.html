Salad: Chicken-and-Fruit Salad

Chicken-and-Fruit Salad

 

3/4 cup orange marmalade

3 Tbsp soy sauce

3 Tbsp lemon juice

1 1/2 Tbsp chopped fresh ginger

6 skinned and boned chicken breast halves

1 cored fresh pineapple

1 large jicama (optional)

Vegetable cooking spray

2 cups fresh strawberry halves

1 cup fresh raspberries

Orange-Raspberry Vinaigrette

Lettuce leaves

 

Combine first 4 ingredients; remove 1/4 cup marmalade mixture, and chill. 

Place chicken in a shallow dish or a ziploc bag; pour remaining marmalade mixture over chicken. Chill 8 hours. 

Cut pineapple into spears and peel jicama and cut into 1/2 inch slices.

Place pineapple and jicama into a ziploc; pour 1/4 marmalade mixture fruit. Chill 8 hours.

Remove chicken and fruit from marinade and drain.  Discard marinade. Spray chicken and pineapple mixture with cooking spray. 

Grill chicken over medium-high heat 5-6 minutes on each side or until done. Grill pineapple and jicama 2-3 minutes each side.  

Cut chicken and jicama into thin sprips; cut pineapple into bite-size pieces. 

Combine chicken, jicama, pineapple, strawberry halves, and raspberries, toss with Orange-Raspberry Vinaigrette. Serve over lettuce leaves. 

 

Orange-Raspberry Vinaigrette

 

1/2 cup orange marmalade

1/4 cup raspberry vinegar

1 medium jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced

2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh cilantro

2 Tbsp olive oil


Whisk together all ingredients in a small bowl.
