Salad: Cole Slaw

This recipe is from Sugar Buster's cook book.  I use it lots and originally gave the recipe to Mom.  She notes that the amount of apple cider vinegar was "too much, 2-20-01).  When I make it, I add the dressing slowly to make sure it absorbs well.  You may not use all the dressing.

 

Cole Slaw

 

1 red or sweet onion

4 c. cole slaw mix

1/2 c. (about) parsley (Mom notes she used dried)

3 T canola oil

1/4 c. apple cider vinegar

2 packets sweetener

1 t. salt

 

Chop onions.  Combine onion, cabbage, parsley in large bowl.  

 

In small bowl, mix oil, vinegar, sweetener, and salt.  Pour dressing over veggies and toss well.
