Salad: Layered Salad

This is Eleanor Haley's recipe.  That salad was delicious!!  A variation follows that is untitled.


 Layered Salad


1 head lettuce broken into pieces

1 c. celery, chopped

4 eggs, boiled and sliced

1 pkg. peas

1/2 c. green pepper, diced

sweet onions

8 bacon slices

 

Put in layers in order given.  Top with 2 c. mayo blended with 2 tbsp. sugar.  Spread over top.  Finish with 4 oz. cheddar cheese.  

 

 

This is the second, untitled version. 

 

1 head of lettuce broken into pieces

1 can Le Seur peas, drained

1 medium onion, chopped or diced

1 lb. cooked bacon

Parmesan cheese

1 cup mayonnaise mixed with 1 tbsp. sugar


Layer salad:  lettuce, onion, peas; repeat ending with lettuce on top.  


Spread mayonnaise and sugar mixture over salad (like frosting a cake).


Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and cooked bacon crumbled on top.  


Refrigerate 4 or more hours before serving.
