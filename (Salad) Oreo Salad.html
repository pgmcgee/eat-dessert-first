Salad: Oreo Salad

This is from Mary Snow, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Oreo Salad

 

1-20 oz. can crushed pineapple (drained)

1 cup coconut

1 cup walnuts

12 oz. carton cottage cheese

8 oz. whipped topping

1/2 cup chopped cherries

1 cup miniature marshmallows

 

Mix above.  Chill well before adding 1 dozen or more crushed Oreo cookies.  

 

For dessert:  Put in square Pyrex pan and freeze - cut in squares.
