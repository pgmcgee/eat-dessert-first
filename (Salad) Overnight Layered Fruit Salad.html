Salad: Overnight Layered Fruit Salad

This is from an old newspaper article; can't determine a date.

 

Overnight Layered Fruit Salad 

 

2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce

2 Golden Delicious apples

2 navel oranges

2 cups seedless green grapes

1/3 cup mayonnaise

1/3 cup sour cream

1 cup shredded mild cheddar cheese

 

Spread lettuce on bottom of 2-quart serving dish.  Core and quarter apples; slice thinly over lettuce.  Peel and section oranges; squeeze a teaspoon or so of orange juice onto apples; arrange sectioned oranges on top of apples.  Layer grapes.  Combine mayonnaise and sour cream in small bowl; spread over grapes.  Sprinkle shredded cheese over all.  Cover dish tightly with plastic wrap.  Refrigerate overnight.  

 

Makes 6 servings.
