Salad: Pasta Salad

Mom credits this one to La and Sis!  

 

La and Sis' Pasta Salad

 

1 whole bottle Kraft House Italian

1 lb. Spiral Penne pasta or Bow Tie

tomatoes

broccoli

carrots

cauliflower

vidalia onion


Cook pasta and toss all together.  Chill at least 2 - 3 hours.


Add grated cheese; can also add cube cheeses
