Red, White and Blueberry Salad


Red Layer

1   3-ounce package strawberry gelatin

1.25 cups boiling water

.5 cup pineapple juice (from drained pineapple below)

1 cup finely chopped fresh strawberries


Dissolve the gelatin in the boiling water.  Add juice.  When cool, stir in strawberries and pour into a 9 x 9 by 2-inch pan.  Refrigerate until firmly set.  


White Layer


1   3-ounce package lemon gelatin

1   cup boiling water

1   cup whipping cream

1   3-ounce package cream cheese

1   cup white miniature marshmallows

.5  cup drained crushed pineapple 


Dissolve the gelatin in the boiling water.  Refrigerate until slightly thickened.  Then whip the cream, using your mixer to mix the cream cheese into the whipped cream.  Add the gelatin.  Fold in the marshmallows and pineapple.  Spread over the Red Layer and refrigerate until firmly set.


Blue Layer


1   3-ounce package blackberry gelatin

1   cup boiling water

.75cup grape juice

1   cup blueberries


Dissolve the gelatin in boiling water.  Add the grape juice.  Refrigerate.  Watch the mixture carefully, and when it just begins to set up, stir in the blueberries.  Pour over the white layer.  Refrigerate until firmly set.


To serve, cut into squares.  Arrange some of the squares red side up and some of the squares blue side up.  Garnish with strawberries and blueberries.  Add a dollop of whipped cream to each square, if desired.
