Red, white and blue fruit salad

 

1 pt. strawberries

1pt. blueberries

      Melon balls from the heart of half a watermelon

2 bananas, cut into bit-size chunks

      orange juice

 

The red and blue fruit can be prepared in advance, mixed and chilled.  Soak banana chunkc separately in orange and add at serving time.

 

Makes 6 to 8 servings.
