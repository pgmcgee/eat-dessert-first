Salad: Salad a la Pepin

This one is from Ann Spies Batteiger, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Salad a la Pepin

 

2 small heads of lettuce, torn into bite size pieces

2 medium tomatoes, quartered

1/2 cup Spanish olives, sliced

1/4 cup or more grated Romano cheese

2 oz. cottonseed oil

2 oz. wine vinegar

 

Put lettuce in large salad bowl.  Add tomatoes and olives.  Sprinkle cheese over salad.  Toss and serve in chilled bowl with oil and vinegar.
