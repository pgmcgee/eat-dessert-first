Salad: Spinach Salad

This is from Helen Shoemaker.

 

Spinach Salad

 

Dressing:

 

1/2 cup oil

1/4 cup wine vinegar

1/4 cup sugar

1/6 cup ketchup

dash of salt

 

Blend all above well; refrigerate and pour over greens before serving..Top with chopped hard boiled eggs and crisp chopped bacon.  

 

Wash, dry and tear into pieces, a 1 lb. bag of fresh spinach.  

 

You can add small amounts of water chestnuts, thinly sliced onions, sliced mushrooms, and/or bean sprouts.
