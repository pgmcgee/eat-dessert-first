Salad: Three Bean

The handwritten version of this one may have shown a little family "stress"!  Nana started writing it but about 3/4 of it is in Mom's handwriting.  At the end, Mom writes, "(Nana said she likes celery in it and she didn't use green pepper in that you had at Easter) April, 1989".  I can almost hear it as I write it!!  

 

3 Bean Salad

 

1 can (1 lb.) cut green beans, drained

1 can (1 lb.) cut wax beans, drained

1 can (1 lb.) kidney beans, drained

1 onion; sliced and separated into rings

1 green pepper; sliced

3/4 c. sugar

2/3 c. vinegar (-)

1/3 c. salad oil

1 tsp. salt

1 tsp. pepper

 

Combine vegetables.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over vegetables.

 

Cover and chill overnight.  

 

To serve, coat all well with marinade, then drain throughly.

 

Makes 6 to 8 servings.
