Salad: Wilted Lettuce Salad

Wilted Lettuce Salad

 

1 head lettuce broken into bite size pieces

1/2 c. sliced fresh mushrooms

6 slices bacon

1/4 c. + 1 tsp. vinegar

1 1/2 tsp. sugar

1/4 tsp. salt and pepper

 

Put lettuce and mushrooms in bowl.  

 

Cook bacon.  Reserve 3 tbsp. drippings in skillet.  Add vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper to drippings; bring to a boil, stir until sugar dissolves.  

 

Crumble bacon in lettuce.  Pour dressing over lettuce.   Toss and serve immediately.
