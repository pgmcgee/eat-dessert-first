Salads: Our Chinese Chicken Salad

Our Chinese Chicken Salad

 

1/2 bag cole slaw

1/2 bag broccoli slaw

1/2 bag romaine lettuce

2 pkgs Ramen (chicken flavor)

grilled chicken 

scallions

mandarin oranges

snow peas

red peppers

toasted almonds or sunflower seeds

Anything else you want.


Dressing:

1/2 cup olive oil

2-3 Tbsp balsamic

1/3 cup sugar

2 pkgs seasoning from Ramen noodles

mandarin orange juice from bottle or can to taste

 

Toss, Serve and Enjoy!
