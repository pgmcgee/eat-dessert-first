Sauces and Marinades: Phil Gearing's

This was written on the back of a Zayre receipt dated September 11, 1968.  The total charge was $.97 and $.04 cents tax for a grand total of $1.01.  I also remember Phil (Dad's salesman) using a bottle of Italian dressing on a cheap, tough steak and it was delicious!

 

Phil's Marinade
