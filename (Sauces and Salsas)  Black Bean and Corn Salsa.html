Sauces and Salsas: Black Bean and Corn Salsa

This is Laurie and Brian's salsa.  Mom dated it 04-06.  I'm sure Mom tasted it on a visit from them at The Villages. 

 

Black Bean and Corn Salsa

 

1 can black beans, drained

1 can corn, drained

1 container of salso or pico de gallo

 

Combine and serve with Scoops or in a quesadilla with leftover chicken or meat (add grated cheese).
