Sauces: White Sauces

These two white sauce recipes are from Mom's home ec training at the University of Miami.  They were neatly written on  3x5" index cards in pencil.  I don't know if she ever made them or the cards were used for studying for a test!

 

White Sauce No. 1

 

2 tbsp. shortening

2 tbsp. flour

1 c. milk

1/4 tsp. salt

cayenne (red pepper)

 

This makes a medium thick white sauce and is used for creamed vegetables.  

 

 

White Sauce No. 2

 

2 tbsp. shortening 

1 1/2 tbsp. flour

1 c. milk

salt

cayenne

 

This makes a slgihtly thinner sauce than White Sauce No. 1 and is for scalloped dishes.
