Seafood: Basil Baked Triggerfish

Basil Baked Triggerfish

 

1 lb. Triggerfish

1 T. margarine

1 T. lemon juice; fresh

1/4 tsp. onion powder

1/2 tsp. dried basil

1/2 bell pepper, sliced

 

Melt margarine and add juice and pour over fish.  Sprinkle with spices.  Garnish with pepper slices.  Cover and bake at 375 for 10 - 15 minutes.
