Mom copied this in July, 2005 during her visit with the Batteiger's in Jacksonville.

 

Bo and Ann's Cedar Planked Salmon

 

1 cedar plank

1/2 c. Sun-dried Tomato Vinaigarette Dressing (Kraft)

1/4 c. chopped fresh parsley

1/4 c. sun dried tomatoes

1 Tbsp. vegetable oil

1 salmon fillet (2 lbs.), 1" thick, skin removed
