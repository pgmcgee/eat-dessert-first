Seafood: Crab Louis

This is Nana's recipe.

 

Crab Louis

 

1/2 c. mayonnaise

1/2 c. sour cream

2 T. chili sauce

2 T. salad oil

1 T. prepared horseradish

1 T. vinegar

1 T. fresh lemon juice

1 T. snipped parsley

2 t. grated onion

1/2 t. salt

4 drops Tabasco sauce

1 lb. frozen crab meat (can use fresh crab meat or frozen Alaska King)

1 medium head of lettuce

watercress

lemon wedges


In a small bowl, mix together all ingredients through Tabasco sauce.  Cover and refreigerate.  


Thaw crab meat, dry on paper towels and break into bite size pieces.


Shred lettuce.  Mound crab meat over the lettuce and top with sauce.


Garnish with watercress and lemon wedges.
