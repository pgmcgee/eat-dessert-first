Inez' Baked Crab

 

1 lb. crab

1 green pepper

4 parsley sprigs (if desired)

1/2 small onion

salt

cayenne pepper

4 celery stalks

4 hard boiled eggs (2 if desired)

6 slices of toast,grated

1 T. Worcestershire sauce

1 cup mayonnaise

1 cup heavy white sauce

 

Combine all ingredients and mix with heavy white sauce.  Add toast crumbs saving a few for top of crabs.  Add 1 cup mayonnaise. 

 

Fill crab shells and top with buttered crumbs.

 

Bake about 20 minutes in hot oven.
