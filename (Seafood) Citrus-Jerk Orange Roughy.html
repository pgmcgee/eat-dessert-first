Seafood: Citrus-Jerk Orange Roughy

Mom copied this in July, 2005 while at the Batteiger's in Jacksonville.

 

Citrus-Jerk Orange Roughy

 

4 orange roughy fillets

1 Tbsp. + 1 tsp. jerk seasoning

1 tsp. olive oil

4 Tbsp. orange marmalade

3 Tbsp. water

2 Tbsp. lemon juice

1/8 tsp. pepper

 

Rub fish with seasonings.  Spray pan, add oil and get hot.  Add fish and cook 3 minutes on each side until the fish flakes.  Remove from skillet.  Add marmalade and other ingredients.
