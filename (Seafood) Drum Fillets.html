Seafood: Drum Fillets

Chipper Drum 

 

2 lbs. drum fillets

1/2 c. creamy Caesar salad dressing

1 c. crushed potato chips

1/2 c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

 

Dip fish in salad dressing.  Place in greased pan.  Combine chips and cheese.  Sprinkle over fish.  Bake in 500 degree oven for 10 minutes. 

 

Serves 6
