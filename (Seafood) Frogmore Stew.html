Seafood: Frogmore Stew

Here's one from Mom's beach suggestions.  Most Batteiger's use Old Bay as the seafood boil of choice.  A homemade variety follows the recipe.

 

 

Frogmore Stew

 

 

1/4 c. Seafood Boil

2 lb. hot smoked sausage links cut into 2" pieces (Use Kielbasa if you can't find hot sausage)

12 ears corn

4 lbs. shrimp

 

Combine Seafood Boil in 6 qts. water.  Bring to boil.  Add sausage; boil 5 minutes.

 

Add corn; cook 5 minutes longer.

 

Add shrimp.  Cook until pink.

 

Drain and serve. 

 

Seafood Boil

 

1/4 c. mustard seeds

2 Tbsp. whole black peppers

2 Tbsp. crushed red hot pepper

6 bay leaves

1 Tbsp. celery seed

1 Tbsp. corriander seeds

1 Tbsp. ginger

few blades of mace (nutmeg can be substituted)

1/4 c. sea salt

 

Combine all.
