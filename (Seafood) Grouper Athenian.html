Seafood: Grouper Athenian

Mom copied this July, 2005 while at The Batteiger's, Jacksonville

 

Grouper Athenian

 

4 grouper fillets

2 tsp. salt-free Greek seasoning

1 (10 oz.) pkg. chopped spinach; thaw and squeeze dry

1 plum tomato, chopped

1/4 c. (1 oz.) crumbled basil and tomato feta cheese

 

Spray pan with Pam.  Rub fish with seasoning and cook 3 minutes on medium high.  Remove skillet from heat, turn fish; top with spinach, tomato and cheese.  Return to heat; cover and cook 3 - 4 minutes until spinach is hot and fish flakes easily.
