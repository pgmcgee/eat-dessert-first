Seafood: Honey-Balsamic Salmon

This is one of Ann Batteiger's recipes, written by Mom on an Eagle Harbor note pad.

 

Grilled Honey-Balsamic Salmon

 

2 6 oz. salmon steaks, 1/2" thick

1 1/2 T. honey

1 1/2 T. Dijon

1 T. balsamic vinegar

1/4 tsp. pepper

1/4 tsp. garlic salt

 

Combine all ingredients in bowl and brush over fish.  Place on grill at medium heat (350 - 400).  Grill covered 2 - 3 minutes.
