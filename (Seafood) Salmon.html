Salmon in One Skillet

 

4 oz. salmon

salt and pepper to taste

3 Tbsp. soy sauce

3 Tbsp. orange juice

1/2 tsp. dark Sesame oil

 

Cook fish 3 minutes, uncovered, on each side.  Cove and cook additional 3 minutes.  Take out of pan.

Add soy and OJ to skillet. Cook 1 minute to deglaze skillet.  Add oil, stir well.  Pour sauce over fish.
