Seafood: Shrimp Creole

This recipe is dated by Mom on 8-7-56.  She describes it as "delicious".  She was a bit "skimpy" on her proportions!!!

 

Shrimp Creole

 

cooked shrimp

2 tbsp. fat

minced onion

minced green pepper

butter

1 can tomato paste

1 can water 

 dash sugar, salt and chili powder


Melt fat.  Add onion and green pepper.  Brown slightly.  Add tomato paste and water.  Put in butter, sugar, salt and chili powder.  


Simmer for quite a while until sauce thickens.


Add the shrimp and cook a little longer.  


Serve over rice.
