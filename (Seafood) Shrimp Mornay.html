Seafood: Shrimp Mornay

This is one of Mom's beach recipes.

 

 

Shrimp Mornay

 

 

1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced

2 green onion, sliced (Mom recommends using more)

2 Tbsp. margarine

1 lb. raw, medium shrimp, shelled

3 Tbsp. dry white wine

1 8-oz. pkg. cream cheese, cubed

3/4 c. milk

1/2 c. (2 oz.) shredded Swiss cheese

 

Saute mushrooms and onions in margarine.  Add shrimp and wine; cook 2 - 3 minutes or until shrimp are pink. Remove veggies and shrimp from pan with slotted spoon; add cream cheese and milk to pan.


Stir over low heat with wire whisk until smooth.  Add Swiss cheese; stir until cheese is melted.


Return shrimp and veggies to pan; mix lightly.  


Serve over rice or pasta.  


Total cooking time about 10 minutes.


4 servings
