Seafood: Stuffed Flounder

Dated 9/9/90.  Mom's comment, underlined twice was, "This was good!!.  She has another comment at the end of the recipe.


Stuffed Flounder Perdido


Flounder

Crab Meat Stuffing 

Browned BButter

salt, pepper, paprika

dash Tabasco

Paprika


     Place Flounder in Pam(ed) baking dish.  Salt and pepper flounder.  Spread crab meat stuffing over top.  Season if needed.  Put browned butter over stuffing.  Place other piece of Flounder on top.  Season - add little butter and paprika.


     Bake at 375 until fish flakes.

                                                                                                                  "Delicious!!"
