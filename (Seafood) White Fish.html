This was in a book I found today (March 4, 2008) while going through Mom's treasures.  It's a little notebook that tells us these are recipes she took with her to the beach.  

 

 

White Fish

 from Regis and Kathie Lee Live, April, 1989

 

 

4 pieces white fish (preferably grouper!)

salt and pepper

lemon juice

1 Tbsp. olive oil

2 Tbsp. pickle relish

2 Tbsp. parsley or cilantro

1/2 c. mayonnaise

cayenne pepper

1/2 tsp. lemon juice  


Mix all the above and add to 2 beaten egg whites.  


Bake fish for 15 minutes at 450.


Put egg white mixture over fish (cooled).  Bake for 5 min. longer.


Note:  Non-Fat Plain Yogurt can be substituted for the mayonnaise.
