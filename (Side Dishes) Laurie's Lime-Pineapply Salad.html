Side Dishes: Laurie's Lime-Pineapply Salad

This is one I remember from most holidays!   The recipe is dated November 23, 1999 with the note from Mom, "Finally wrote it down!".  Her other note is that you can add chopped pecans and some diced celery.  But she didn't!.



Laurie's Lime-Pineapple Salad


1 sm. pkg. Lime Jello

1 c. hot water

1 pkg. Cream Cheese

1 or more sm. can pineapple


Dissolve Jello in hot water.  Stir in cream cheese until melted (use whisk to get lumps out).  Pour into mold and refrigerate overnight.
