Sides: Broccoli-Rice Casserole

This is from Marie Zachary, copied by Mom.

 

Broccoli-Rice Casserole 

 

1/2 stick margarine

1/2 cup chopped onion

1 cup cooked rice

1 can Cream of Chicken soup

1 can Cream of Mushroom soup

1 cup grated Cheddar cheese

1 pkg. frozen chopped broccoli; cooked and drained

1 tsp. salt

 

Cook rice.  Cook broccoli and drain.  Mix all ingredients in bowl.  Pour into 9" x 13" dish and cook at 350 about 30 minutes.

Cheese Broccoli Casserole 

 

1 pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, cooked and drained.

2 cups cooked rice

1 can Cream of Celery soup (undiluted)

1 small jar Cheez Whiz


Mix all ingredients together.  Put in 2 quart casserole.  Bake at 350 until bubbly.
