Sides: Scalloped Potatoes

This is one of my favorites and should be enjoyed with meatloaf!  It needs some work to get the knack.  I tried it recently without success.  I think one issue was the potato slices.  Make sure slices are even. 

 

Mom's Scalloped Potatoes

 

6 medium potatoes

 

Peel and slice evenly.

 

2 tbsp. butter 

2 tbsp. flour

2 c. milk

1 1/2 tsp. salt

 

Layer the potatoes in a pan.  Dot with butter and flour while layering.  Pour in milk and add salt.  

 

Make sure potatoes are covered.  Use more milk or water to cover.

 

Bake at 350 for one hour.
