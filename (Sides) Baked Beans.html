Baked Beans


1.  Wash and drain 1 lb. of Navy beans.


2.  Place in thrift cooker with beans, 4 tablespoons molasses, 1/4 cup brown sugar, one onion cut fine, 1/2 # (lb.),  1 tablespoon prepared mustard and four cups cold water.  Mix well.


3.  Cover and switch to low and cook 12 hours.

 

4.  Just before time to take them up, season with tomato catsup to suit taste.


What's a thrift cooker?  Hotpoint devised it to replace a regular burner on the range.  It had three levels...boiling, simmer and warming.  It is circa 1936.  Apparently, it wasn't a big hit!!  At least it didn't pass the test of time!!
