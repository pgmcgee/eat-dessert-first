Sides: Broccoli Casserole

Broccoli Casserole

 

1 can cream of mushroom soup

1 cup grated cheese

1 scant cup mayo

2 eggs, well beaten

2 pkgs. chopped broccoli

Ritz crackers, crumbled


Cook broccoli for 5 minutes; drain well.  Combine eggs, soup, cheese, and mayo in bowl.  Fold in broccoli.  pour into buttered casserole and sprinkle with crackers.  


Dot with butter and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
