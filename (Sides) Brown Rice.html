Sides: Brown Rice

This is Della Wright's version of a family tradition.  Mom notes that she last edited the recipe on April 15, 2006 when she added the proper pan size.

 

Brown Rice  

 

1 stick butter

3 small onions, chopped

 

Saute onions in melted butter.

 

Add:

1 cup raw rice

2 cans consomme

 

Bake in 11" x 7" pan in 350 oven, uncovered, for 1 hour.

 

Serves 6 generously. 

 

 

This version is from Henrietta, August, 1969.  

 

Rice Consomme 

 

1 stick margarine

1 cup raw rice

1 can beef consomme soup

1 can water

Sprinkle of dehydrated onions (optional)

 

Mix all.  Cover and cook 1 hour at 350. 

 

This can be doubled.  Add 1 can onion soup mix to the water.
