Sides: Cornbread Dressing

This is Mom's "perfected" cornbread dressing recipe. She wrote it down as good in 2002, 2003 and 2006.  A variation from Thanksgiving, 2001 follows.

 

Mom's Cornbread Dressing

 

8 slices white bread

6 c. corn bread

4 c. chicken broth (canned)

1 c. finely chopped onion

salt and pepper

1/4 c. melted butter

 

Put bread out to dry.  Make cornbread by package directions.  Combine all ingredients and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

 

 

Cornbread Dressing variant

 

4 c. crumbled cornbread

2 c. crumbled toast crumbs

4 1/2 c. chicken, pork or turkey stock

1/2 c. onion, chopped fine

 

Mix cornbread with bread crumbs and add boiling stock.  Cover and set for 5 - 10 minutes.  Add onion.  Bake in well greased pan at 375 for 30 - 40 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper as needed.

 

Makes 8 cups
