Couscous with Peas and Feta

 

1 1/2 cups water

1 cup uncooked couscous

1/4 cup olive oil

1 tsp grated lemon rind

1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice

3/4 tsp salt

1/8 tsp ground red pepper

3/4 cup frozen green sweet peas, thawed

1/4 purple onion, thinly sliced

1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

1/4 cup chopped fresh mint

2 Tbsp toasted pine nuts

 

Boil 1 1/2 cups water, remove from heat and stir in couscous. Let stand 10 minutes. Transfer to large bowl. 

Stir together olive oil, lemon rind, and next 3 ingredients; add to couscous and stir with a fork. 

Toss peas, sliced onion, crumbled feta, and chopped mint gently into couscous; cover and chill at least 2 hours. Stir in pine nuts just before serving. 

 

Made: 6-2007 in NYC
