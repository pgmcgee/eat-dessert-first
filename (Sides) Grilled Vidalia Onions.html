Sides: Grilled Vidalia Onions

This is from Dave and Roxanne, the neighbors at Lake Lanier.  Mom and Dad loved these onions.  Dave would often put two extra on the grill for them.


Vidalia Onions


small to medium size Vidalia onions (one for each guest)

butter

beef bouillon cube - cut in half

parmesan cheese

foil


Peel and core small hole in top of onion (I use an apple corer)


Place a dab of butter in cored onion


Place 1/2 beef bouillon cube in butter


Sprinkle as much Permesan cheese on top.  Replace cored piece of onion on top


Wrap tightly in foil


Bake or grill onions for 15 - 20 minutes or until done.  When knife inserted through foil is soft to the touch.
