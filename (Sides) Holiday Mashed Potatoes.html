Sides: Holiday Mashed Potatoes

This one is from Eleanor Haley


Holiday Mashed Potatoes


12 medium potatoes

1 pkg. cream cheese - 8 oz. (room temperature)

1/4 c. butter

1/2 c. sour cream

1/2 c. milk

2 eggs - lightly beaten

1/4 c. chopped onions

1 tsp. salt

dash pepper


In large mixing bowl mash hot potatoes.  Add cream cheese in small pieces and butter.  Beat well until both are completely mixed.  Mix in sour cream.


To milk, add eggs and onion.  Add this to potato mixture.  Season with salt and pepper.


Beat wll until light and fluffy.  Pour in greased casserole.  Bake covered for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
