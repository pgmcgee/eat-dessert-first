Sides: Macaroni and Cheese

Mom found this recipe in a magazine.  Her comment:  "This is a lot like Mother used to make! 9-6-06"

 

Macaroni and Cheese

 

1 1/2 cups cooked elbow macaroni

1 cup (4 ounces) shredded sharp cheddar cheese

1/2 cup milk

1 egg, lightly beaten

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon butter

 

In a medium bowl, combine the macaroni, cheese, milk, egg and salt.  Mix well.  Pour into greased 1-qt. shallow baking dish; dot with butter.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 30 - 35 minutes or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.

 

Yield:  2 servings
