Parisian Rice


Melt butter in skillet.  Add 1 small can Mushrooms and Pieces and 3/4 c. rice.  Saute.  (Not Minute Rice)


Add 1 can Onion soup and 1/2 can water.  Cover and let stand 30 minutes or until done.
