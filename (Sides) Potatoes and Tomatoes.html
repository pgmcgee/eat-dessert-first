Potatoes and Tomatoes

This is a new version of our favorite "Oven Potatoes"

Cut new potatoes into steak fries. Toss with olive oil and salt and pepper.
Bake at 400 for about 20 minutes.?

Cut plum tomatoes into quarters. Mince some garlic and stir in with tomatoes.?
Add the tomatoes and garlic to the potatoes (after you've cooked potatoes for the
20 minutes). Cook about 10-15 more minutes and top with parmesan cheese when
you take them out of the oven. Serve hot....
