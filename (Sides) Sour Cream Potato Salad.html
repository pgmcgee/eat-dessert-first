Sides: Sour Cream Potato Salad

This is Mary Snow's recipe, St. Petersburg, FL  I think it should be noted that the potatoes need to be pre-cooked!

 

Sour Cream Potato Salad

 

6 potatoes

1 small onion, minced     can also add celery if desired

2 tsp. salt

4 T. sugar

1/4 tsp. black pepper

3 hard boiled eggs

2 T. vinegar

1 c. sour cream

1/2 tsp. dry mustard

6 bacon slices, crumbled 

 

Slice potatoes thin, add onion, 2 eggs.  Then all ingredients except mustard.  Stir.  Add mustard and mix again.  

 

Use other egg and bacon for garnish.

 

Add celery for crunch!
