Squash-Zucchini Casserole


3 tsp. olive oil

3 medium yellow squash, sliced thin

3 medium zucchini, sliced thin

1/2 purple onion

1/2 sweet pepper

1 T. butter

1/2 tsp. garlic powder

salt and pepper to taste

1/2 tsp. Lawry's seasoned salt

Bread crumbs

shredded cheese


Heat oil in skillet.  (Best to use skillet that can be put into oven.)  Add all ingredients and saute 8 - 10 minutes until slightly tender.  Remove from heat.  Put crumbs and cheese to cover top.  Heat in 350 oven 5 - 7 minutes or until cheese melts.
