Sides: Sweet Potato Souffle

This is one of Mom's specialties.  It's served at every Thanksgiving table.

 

 

Brian's Sweet Potato Souffle

 

5 medium size sweet potatoes

1+ cup sugar (taste for sweetness)

2 eggs, beaten

1 stick margarine

 

Boil sweet potatoes until tender.  Peel while hot (hold with a fork to prevent burning!)

 

Put margarine, sweet potatoes, eggs and sugar in pan or bowl and beat with electric beater until smooth.  

 

Put in casserole dish sprayed with Pam.  Cover with large marshmallows.  

 

Bake at 350 for 20 to 30 minutes or until done.  

 

Enjoy!


Comments from Sis after Thanksgiving 2009:

I used a little less than a cup of sugar and they were plenty sweet.  Make sure you bake the casserole until the edges are slightly brown and then add the marshmallows for the last few minutes.  Sweet Potato Souffle takes closer to 45 minutes to one hour to bake.
