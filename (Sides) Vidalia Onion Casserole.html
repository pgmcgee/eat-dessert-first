Sides: Vidalia Onion Casserole

I can vouch for this one.  It is delicious!!  The next-door neighbor at the lake, Roxann, shared this with Mom.  

 

Vidalia Onion Casserole

 

10 oz. noodles

2 pkgs. Gouda cheese, shredded

2 0r 3 large Vidalia onions

1/2 cup flour

2 tsp. salt

1/2 cup butter

3 1/2 cups milk

3/4 cup Parmesan cheese

bread crumbs

 

Saute onions in butter.  Remove from butter, add flour, salt, milk to butter and cook until it thickens.

 

Boil noodles until they are halfway cooked, drain.

 

Layer two times in this order:

 

noodles

onions

cheese

sauce

 

Sprinkel Parmesan on top and add more sauce.

 

Sprinkle bread crumbs over all.

 

Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

 

You can add ham and/or green peppers.
