Soup: Black-Eyed Pea Chowder

This one is Ann Batteiger's, dated January 3, 1993.

 

Ann's Black-Eyed Pea Chowder

 

1/2 lb. bacon, diced

2 cups celery, chopped

2 cups green pepper, chopped

2 cups onion, chopped

1 - 10.5 ounce can beef consomme

2 - 16 ounce cans tomatoes

2 - 16 ounce cans black-eyed peas

 

Saute bacon with celery, green pepper and onion.  Add consumme, tomatoes and peas.  Simmer 30 - 40 minutes OR

Put all ingredients in crock pot and cook on low about 8 hours.

 

8 servings.
