Soup: Onion

This recipe is from Betty DeGroot, St. Petersburg, FL

 

Onion Soup

 

1 c. chopped or sliced onions.....saute' in

1 1/2 tbsp. butter (slowly)...........stir in

1 1/2 tsp. flour and

5 c. beef bouillon (5 cubes + 5 cups water)


Cook 1 hour in oven


Season with: 


1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

salt and pepper


Top with toasted French bread slices.  Sprinkle with 1/4 c. shredded Swiss cheese,1/4 c. grated Parmesan and 1/4 c. shredded Mozzarella.  (Okay to use only two kinds of cheese.  Increase the amount of each to make total 3/4 c.)
