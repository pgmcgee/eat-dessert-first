Soup: Vegetable

Mom dates this 1980 and writes:  "Mine that Bo likes".....Enjoy!   It's one of her classics!!

 

Mom's Vegetable Soup

 

brown meat

add can tomatoes, rinse can with water

add can creamed corn

add package of mixed vegetables

season with salt and pepper

 

If you don't have mixed vegetables, add carrots, celery, onions, potatoes, cabbage, okra
