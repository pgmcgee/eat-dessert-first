Piggy Pudding


16 link pork sausages

4 5 tart applies - peeled and sliced

7.5 oz. pkg. corn bread mix - prepared


Pre-heat oven to 450.  Cook sausages and place in 9" square baking dish.  Layer sliced apples on top.  Pour corn bread batter over all and bake about 30 minutes or until corn bread is done.  Serve with warm maple syrup,  Mom then comments:  (ugh!)  HA!
