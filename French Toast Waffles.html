French Toast Waffles

A new favorite recipe for us!  Had to make two batches of the milk mixture to use the entire loaf of bread. Made this for the French Open final last weekend, and everybody LOVED it!! Of course, the Mimosas weren't bad either :)

 

Ingredients

cooking spray

1 cup skim milk

1 tablespoon sugar

1 tablespoon butter, melted

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

2 large eggs

1 loaf of day-old French bread, sliced 1/2-1 inch thick*

 

Coat waffle iron with cooking spray, and preheat.

Combine milk and the next 5 ingredients (milk through eggs), stirring well with a whisk. Place bread in a 13x9 baking dish; pour milk mixture over bread, turning to coat pieces. Let stand 5 minutes.

Place 4 bread slices on hot waffle iron. Cook 3-5 minutes, or until done; repeat with remaining bread.

Serve with fresh berries, powdered sugar, or maple syrup.

 

*Make sure to use slightly hardened bread so that it won't be soggy.
