Reflections

Mom wrote this on the back of a lime green sheet of paper on which she had partially written Marie Zachary's recipe for Chicken Pie.

 

She wrote that it is a quote from Aunt Grace's husband.  Aunt Grace is 80 years old.  (This saying was not dated but had to be from the St. Pete era.)

 

 

The past is history,

the future is mystery,

and the present is a gift.

 

That's why it's called the present!!
